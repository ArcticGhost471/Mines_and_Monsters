Sculk Catalyst

Armor Class 5

Speed 0 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
1 (-5) 1 (-5) 12 (+1) 1 (-5) 3 (-4) 1 (-5)

Damage Immunities necrotic

Condition Immunities blinded, charmed, deafened, frightened

Senses blindsight 30 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 6

Languages —

Challenge 0 (10 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Reactions

Bloom. When a creature dies within 30 feet of the catalyst, the catalyst begins absorbing the creature's body and soul. At the end of the catalyst's next turn, if the body is still within 30 feet of the catalyst and the catalyst remains intact, the body is consumed. A creature whose body is consumed in this way can't be restored to life by any means short of a wish spell unless the catalyst is destroyed. When the catalyst consumes a creature, it blooms, causing sculk to spread outward in a 5-foot radius. As the sculk spreads, sculk sensors and sculk shriekers (see their respective statblocks) have a chance of of forming in the newly corrupted terrain, at the DM's discretion.
