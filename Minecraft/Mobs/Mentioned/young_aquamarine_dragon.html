Actions

Multiattack. The dragon makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.


Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10 + 4) piercing damage plus 5 (1d10) Cold or Fire damage (the dragon's choice).


Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.


Breath Weapon (Recharge 5-6). The dragon uses one of the following breath weapons.


Water Breath. The dragon exhales water in a 30-foot cone, or a 60-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in the chosen area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 49 (9d10) Fire or Cold damage (the dragon's choice) on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Flood Breath. The dragon expels water in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw. On a failed save, the creature is pushed 40 feet away from the dragon and is knocked prone.
