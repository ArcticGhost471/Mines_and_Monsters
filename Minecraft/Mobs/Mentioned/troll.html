Keen Smell. The troll has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on smell.


Regeneration. The troll regains 5 hit points at the start of its turn. If the troll takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the troll's next turn. The troll dies only if it starts its turn with 0 hit points and doesn't regenerate.


Solar Petrification. While in sunlight, the troll must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw at the end of each of its turns. On a failed save, the troll begins to turn to stone and is restrained. It must repeat the saving throw at the end of its next turn. On a success, the effect ends. On a failure, the troll is petrified until freed by the greater restoration spell or other magic.


Actions

Multiattack. The troll makes three attacks: one with its bite and two with its claws.


Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.


Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) slashing damage.
