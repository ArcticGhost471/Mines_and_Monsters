Legendary Actions

Herobrine can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Herobrine regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Teleport. Herobrine uses his Teleport action.

Unarmed Strike. Herobrine makes an unarmed strike.

Frightening Gaze (Costs 2 Action). Herobrine fixes his gaze on a creature it can see within 120 feet of him. The creature must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw against this magic or be frightened for 1 minute. The frightened target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to Herobrine's gaze for the next 24 hours.
