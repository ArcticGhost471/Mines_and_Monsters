Evil's Bane. The sentinel can magically sense the presence of evil-aligned creatures up to 60 feet away. It knows the general direction they're in but not their exact locations. Its weapon attacks are considered magical for the purpose of overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks when targeting such creatures.


Iron Restoration. A creature within 10 feet of the sentinel can use its action to offer it a piece of refined iron. The iron is magically consumed and heals the sentinel, restoring 20 hit points. The sentinel can't benefit from this trait again until it finishes a short or long rest, or until it regains hit points by another means.


Actions

Multiattack. The sentinel makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 5 feet away from the sentinel if it is Huge or smaller.
