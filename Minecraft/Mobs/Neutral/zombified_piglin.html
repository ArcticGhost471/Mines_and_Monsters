Zombified Piglin Template

Only a fiend (piglin) can become a zombified piglin, and only if it remains outside of its plane for 30 minutes. When a piglin becomes a zombified piglin, it retains all its statistics except as noted below.

 Lost Characteristics. The piglin loses its original saving throw and skill bonuses, special senses that aren't darkvision, and special traits. It loses any action that isn't multiattack or a weapon attack.

 Type. The piglin's type changes from fiend to undead, it no longer requires air, food, drink, or sleep, and it retains its tags.

 Alignment. The piglin is neutral.

 Ability Scores. The piglin's ability scores change as follows: Int 4 (-3), Wis 8 (-1), Cha 6 (-2).

 Damage Immunities. The piglin is immune to poison.

 Condition Immunities. The piglin can't be poisoned. It also doesn't suffer from exhaustion.

 Senses. If it doesn't have it already, the piglin has Darkvision with a radius of 60 feet.

 Languages. The piglin can't speak but understands the languages it knew in life.

 New Reaction: Hivemind. If the piglin is hit by an attack roll, it calls out to other zombified piglins within a 60-foot radius. Each zombified piglin within range that can hear it can take a Reaction to move up to its Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.
