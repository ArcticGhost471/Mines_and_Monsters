Innate Spellcasting. Herobrine's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 22). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: detect magic, message, prestidigitation

2/day each: animate dead, creation, dominate beast

1/day each: mirage arcane, scrying


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Herobrine fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. Herobrine has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. Herobrine's weapon attacks are magical.


Actions

Multiattack. Herobrine makes three Diamond Pickaxe attacks.


Diamond Pickaxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8 + 6) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage.


Hellfire Charge. Ranged Spell Attack: +13 to hit, range 150 ft., one target. Hit: 33 (6d10) fire damage.


Teleport. Herobrine magically teleports, along with any equipment he is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space he can see.


Legendary Actions

Herobrine can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Herobrine regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Attack. Herobrine makes one hellfire charge attack.

Teleport. Herobrine uses his Teleport action.

Jumpscare (Costs 2 Action). Herobrine teleports in front of a creature it can see within 30 feet of himself. The creature must succeed on a DC 22 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened for 1 minute. The frightened target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to Herobrine's Jumpscare for the next hour.

Wither Flame (Costs 3 Action). Ranged Spell Attack: +13 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d6 + 7) necrotic damage, and the target is afflicted with Wither for 1 minute. While afflicted, the target is poisoned and takes 10 (4d4) necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Herobrine's Lair

Herobrine hides away in select settlements away from sentient life. He may choose to conjure a rudimentary dungeon as a temporary base of operations which he makes hidden as close to his targets as possible. Yet his true bases lie within deep forests or hellish landscapes, from elaborate mansions to grand castles.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Herobrine takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects:

  • Seal the Way. Herobrine seals one doorway or other entryway within the lair. The opening must be unoccupied. It is filled with wooden planks for 1 minute or until Herobrine creates this effect again.
  • Stasis Field. A beacon erupts at a previously designated point in the lair. Herobrine then chooses one creature he can see within 120 feet of himself. That creature must succeed on a DC 22 Charisma saving throw or be teleported to the beacon, be suspended 10 feet in the air, and be incapacitated. The creature remain in that state until initiative count 20 on the next round or Herobrine dies, at which point the creature falls.
  • Undead Servants. Herobrine causes up to six corpses within the lair to rise as skeletons or zombies. These undead obey his telepathic commands, which can reach anywhere in the lair.

Regional Effect

The region containing a Herobrine's lair is warped by his precense, which creates one or more of the following effects:

  • Beast Thralls. Certain beasts like pigs, oxen, sheep, and chickens within 1 mile of Herobrine's lair serve as Herobrine's eyes and ears. Their eyes unnaturally glow white when under Herobrine's control.
  • Eternal Night. A magical dome encompasses the area within 1 mile of the lair, sinking the land into an eternal night.
  • Mists. A creeping fog clings to the ground within 500 feet of Herobrine's lair.

If Herobrine dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.

Spellcasting. Herobrine casts one of the following spells, requiring no material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 22):


At will: Detect Magic, Message, Prestidigitation

2/day each: Animate Dead, Creation, Dominate Beast

1/day each: Mirage Arcane, Scrying


Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, Herobrine can expend a use to take one of the following actions. Herobrine regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.


Jumpscare. Herobrine teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 22, each creature within 10 feet of the space Herobrine appears in. Failure: 10 (3d6) Psychic damage, and the target has the Frightened condition until the end of its next turn. Failure or Success: Herobrine can't take this action again until the start of his next turn.


Flaming Strike. Herobrine moves up to his Speed and makes one Hellfire Charge attack.


Stasis Field. Charisma Saving Throw: DC 22, one creature Herobrine can see within 120 feet. Failure: 24 (7d6) Force damage, the target has the Incapacitated condition until the start of the Herobrine's next turn, and is teleported to an unoccupied space of Herobrine's choice within 120 feet of Herobrine. Failure or Success: Herobrine can't take this action again until the start of his next turn.


Herobrine's Lair

Herobrine secludes himself in settlements away from sentient creatures, whether it be a rudimentary dungeon, an elaborate mansion, or a hellish castle.


Regional Effects

The region containing Herobrine's lair is warped by his presence, creating the following effects:


Beast Thralls. Tiny to Medium Beasts magically gain the ability to understand Herobrine and can communicate telepathically with him while within 1 mile of the lair. These Beasts exhibit glowing white eyes akin to Herobrine's own.

Eternal Night. An eternal night befalls the land within 1 mile of the lair.

Mists. The area within 1 mile of the lair is Lightly Obscured by a persistent, creeping fog. Herobrine and any creatures of its choice are unaffected by the fog.


If Herobrine dies or moves his lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
