Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the mega spud fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Actions

Multiattack. The mega spud makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage.


Fire Outburst (Recharge 4-6). The mega spud launches a 15-foot-radius sphere of fire at a point it can see within 60 feet. Each creature in that area must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (3d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Legendary Actions

The mega spud can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The mega spud regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Move. The mega spud moves up to half its speed.

Potato Shield (Costs 3 Actions). The mega spud forms a magical shield around itself, granting it resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from weapon attacks until the end of its next turn.

Shed Peel (Costs 3 Actions). The mega spud sheds its outer layer and regains 22 (4d8 + 4) hit points.


An Mega Spud's Lair

The mega spud resides at the center of a colosseum-like structure constructed solely out of materials made with potatoes. The colosseum lays abandoned underground, partially flooded at its bottom.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the mega spud takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the dragon can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Nightshade Toxin. A cloud of toxic colorless fumes is released from the colosseum's starch walls and fills a 20-foot-sphere centered on a point the mega spud chooses within 120 feet of it. The cloud spreads around corners and remains until the mega spud dismisses it as an action, uses this lair action again, or dies. Any creature in the cloud must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. A creature that ends its turn in the cloud takes 10 (3d6) poison damage.
  • Swirling Tempest. A 20-foot-radius whirlpool surges at a point the mega spud can see within 120 feet. Each creature in the whirlpool must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 20 feet into it and knocked prone.
  • Tangling Roots. Grasping roots erupt in a 20-foot radius centered on a point in the ground the mega spud can see within 120 feet of it. The area becomes difficult terrain and each creature there must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the roots. A creature can be freed if it or another creature takes an action to make a DC 14 Strength check and succeeds. The roots wilt away when the mega spud uses this lair action again or when the mega spud dies.
