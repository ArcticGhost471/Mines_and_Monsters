Limited Flight. The chicken descends slowly at the end of a turn if it's airborne and the only thing holding it aloft is its flying speed. Descending in this way causes it to take no falling damage.


Sporadic Gemstone Laying. The chicken lays lapiz lazuli every 1d12 hours. Whenever the chicken attempts to lay lapiz lazuli, roll a d20. On a 20, the chicken lays a diamond instead. On a 1, the chicken explodes in a ball of feathers that fills a 5-foot-radius sphere centered on itself and is destroyed.


Actions

Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.
