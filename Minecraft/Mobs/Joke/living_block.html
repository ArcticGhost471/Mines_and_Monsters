Living Matter. While the genius rei remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from the normal natural landscape. A character who succeeds on a DC 22 Survival Check will notice something is amiss, but it's not immediately apparent what.


Rolling Charge. If the genius rei moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a slam attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 7 (2d6) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.


Actions

Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) bludgeoning damage and the target is grappled (escape DC 13). If the target is already grappled by the genius rei, it becomes restrained.
