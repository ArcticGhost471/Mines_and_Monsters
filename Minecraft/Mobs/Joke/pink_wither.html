Immutable Form. The wither is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The wither has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Regeneration. The wither gains 10 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point. If the wither takes force or radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the wither's next turn.


Sugar Rush. A creature within 10 feet of the wither can use its action to offer it 1lb of sugar. The wither consumes the sugar, restores 5 hit points, and gains one additional head (max of 3 heads on one wither). Each head acts independant to each other and give the wither advantage on Wisdom (perception) checks anad on saving throws against being blinded, deafened, stunned, and knocked unconscious. The wither can't benefit from this trait's healing again until it finishes a short or long rest, or until it regains hit points by another means, if it has three heads.


Actions

Multiattack. The wither makes as many faerie skull attacks as it has heads.


Faerie Skull. Ranged Spell Attack: +5 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) necrotic or radiant damage (wither's choice).


Restoring Skull. The wither fires a pink wither skull at a point it can see within 90 feet which then explodes in a ball of bone-based fertilizer in a 30-foot radius sphere. Each non-undead creature in that sphere that isn't the wither regains 9 (2d6 + 2) hit points. In addition, any plant in that area that isn't fully grown becomes fully grown, and any withering plant is rejuvenated and becomes healthy.


Legendary Actions

The wither can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wither regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Faerie Skull. The wither makes one wither skull attack.

Glimmering Shield (Costs 2 Actions). The wither creates a glimmering, magical shield around itself and gains a +2 bonus to AC until the end of the wither's next turn.
