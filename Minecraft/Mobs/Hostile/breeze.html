Standing Leap. The breeze's long jump is up to 60 feet and its high jump is up to 15 feet, with or without a running start.


Whirlwind Armor. The breeze is constantly surrounded by swirling winds which surround its body, causing ranged attack rolls against it to have disadvantage. Spells remain unaffected by this trait.


Actions

Wind Burst. Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (1d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.


Wind Charge (Recharge 4-5). The breeze launches a wind charge which bursts in a 20-foot-radius sphere at a point it can see within 60 feet of it. Each creature in that sphere must make a DC 12 Strength saving throw. On a failure, a target takes 11 (2d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage, is pushed up to 20 feet straight away from the sphere and knocked prone. If the saving throw is successful, the target takes half the bludgeoning damage and isn't flung away or knocked prone.
