Zoglin Template

Only a fiend (hoglin) can become a zoglin, and only if it remains outside of its plane for 30 minutes. When a zoglin becomes a zoglin, it retains all its statistics except as noted below. The hoglin loses any trait, such as Amphibious, that assumes a living physiology.

 Lost Characteristics. The zoglin loses its original saving throw and skill bonuses, and special senses that aren't darkvision. It loses the zombification plague trait and any action that isn't multiattack or a melee weapon attack that deals bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing damage. If it has an action or a melee weapon attack that deals some other type of damage, it loses the ability to deal damage of that type, unless the damage comes from a piece of equipment, such as a magic item.

 Type. The zoglin's type changes from fiend to undead, it no longer requires air, food, drink, or sleep, and it retains its tags.

 Alignment. The zoglin is chaotic evil.

 Ability Scores. The zoglin's ability scores change as follows: Int 1 (-5), Wis 6 (-2), Cha 3 (-4).

 Damage Immunities. The zoglin is immune to poison.

 Condition Immunities. The zoglin can't be poisoned. It also doesn't suffer from exhaustion.

 Senses. If it doesn't have it already, the zoglin has Darkvision with a radius of 60 feet.

Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If the zoglin takes 14 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.
