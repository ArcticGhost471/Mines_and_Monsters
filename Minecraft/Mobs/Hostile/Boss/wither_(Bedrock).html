Death Burst. When the wither dies, it explodes in a burst of ash and decay. Each creature within 30 feet of it must make a DC 23 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Freedom of Movement. The wither ignores difficult terrain, and magical effects can't reduce its speed or cause it to be restrained. It can spend 5 feet of movement to escape from nonmagical restraints or being grappled.


Immutable Form. The wither is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The wither has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Regeneration. The wither gains 40 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point. If the wither takes radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the wither's next turn.


Spawn Burst. When the wither is created, it unleashes a blast of necrotic energy. Each creature within 30 feet of it must make a DC 23 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Siege Monster. The wither deals double damage to objects and structures.


Three Heads. The wither has advantage on Wisdom (perception) checks and on saving throws against being blinded, deafened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.


Wither Armor (Mythic Trait; Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If the wither is reduced to 0 hit points, it doesn't die or become unconscious. Instead, it regains 300 hit points and is enveloped in a magical armor for 1 hour. While armored, its fly speed increases to 60 feet, and it gains immunity to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical ranged attacks. In addition, it summons 3 wither skeletons in unoccupied spaces within 5 feet of it. The skeletons act as its allies and take their turns immediately after the wither in the initiative order. Additionally, the wither can now use the options in the "Mythic Actions" section for 1 hour. Award a party an additional 62,000 XP (124,000 XP total) for defeating the wither after its Wither Armor activates.


Actions

Multiattack. The wither makes four Withering Skull attacks.


Withering Skull. Ranged Spell Attack: +10 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (4d6 + 2) necrotic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 23 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 5 (2d4) Necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Reactions

Grim Harvest. When the wither kills a creature with necrotic damage the wither gains 5 (2d4) hit points.


Legendary Actions

The wither can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wither regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Move. The wither moves up to half its speed.

Withering Skull. The wither makes one withering skull attack.

Desecrated Skull (Costs 2 Actions). The wither launches flaming blue skull that explodes in a 10-foot-radius Sphere of necrotic energy at a point it can see within 90 feet of it. Each creature within that sphere must succeed on a DC 23 Constitution saving throw or take 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. On a failed save, the target is also poisoned until the end of its next turn. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 5 (2d4) necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Mythic Actions

If the wither's Wither Armor trait has activated in the last hour, it can use the options below as legendary actions.


Barrage. The wither makes two wither skull attacks.

Battering Flight (Costs 2 Actions). The wither flies up to its fly speed toward a creature it can see. Each creature within 5 feet of the wither as it moves must succeed on a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or take 21 (6d6) bludgeoning damage, be pushed 10 feet away from the wither, and be knocked prone. The wither can move through spaces occupied by Large or smaller creatures but must end its movement in an unoccupied space.

Traits

Death Burst. The wither explodes when it dies. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 19, each creature in a 30-foot Emanation originating from the wither. Failure: 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage. Success: Half damage.


Desecrating Aura. The area within 20 feet of a wither is Desecrated Ground and all other undead in the area have Advantage on attack rolls. A casting of Dispel Magic on the wither causes this trait to cease functioning for 24 hours.


Immutable Form. The wither can't shape-shift.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The wither has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Regeneration. The wither gains 15 Hit Points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 Hit Point. If the wither takes Force or Radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the wither's next turn.


Spawn Burst. The wither explodes when it becomes animated. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 19, each creature in a 30-foot Emanation originating from the wither. Failure: 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage. Success: Half damage.


Siege Monster. The wither deals double damage to objects and structures.


Actions

Multiattack. The wither makes four Withering Skull attacks.


Withering Skull. Ranged Attack Roll: +10, range 30/120 ft. Hit: 24 (5d6 + 2) Necrotic damage. If the target is a creature, the target has the Poisoned condition for 1 minute. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 7 (2d6) Necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Reactions

Grim Harvest. When the wither kills a creature with necrotic damage the wither gains 5 (2d4) hit points.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3. Immediately after another creature's turn, The wither can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The wither regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.


Desecrated Skull. Dexterity Saving Throw: 23, each creature in a 10-foot-radius Sphere centered on a point the wither can see within 90 feet. Failure: 21 (6d6) Necrotic damage, and the target has the Poisoned condition until the end of its next turn. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 5 (2d4) Necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns. Failure or Success: The wither can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Strafe. The wither moves up to half its speed, and it makes one Withering Skull attack.


Wailing Dash. The wither flies up to its Fly Speed toward a creature it can see. The wither can move through spaces occupied by Large or smaller creatures but must end its movement in an unoccupied space. Each creature within 5 feet of the wither as it moves is targeted once by the following effect. Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 20. Failure: 21 (6d6) bludgeoning damage, be pushed 10 feet away from the wither, and have the Prone condition. Failure or Success: The wither can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.
