Freedom of Movement. The wither ignores difficult terrain, and magical effects can't reduce its speed or cause it to be restrained. It can spend 5 feet of movement to escape from nonmagical restraints or being grappled.


Immutable Form. The wither is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The wither has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Regeneration. The wither gains 40 hit points at the start of its turn if it has at least 1 hit point. If the wither takes force or radiant damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the wither's next turn.


Spawn Burst. When the wither is created, it unleashes a blast of necrotic energy. Each creature within 30 feet of it must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 44 (8d10) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Spectral Wrath. When the wither falls below 150 hit points, it enters a violent frenzy. For the rest of the encounter, the wither gains immunity to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage from nonmagical ranged attacks, and it gains resistance to all other damage from ranged weapon attacks except force, radiant, and thunder damage.


Siege Monster. The wither deals double damage to objects and structures.


Three Heads. The wither has advantage on Wisdom (perception) checks and on saving throws against being blinded, deafened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.


Actions

Multiattack. The wither makes three Withering Skull attacks.


Withering Skull. Ranged Spell Attack: +7 to hit, range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (3d6 + 1) necrotic damage, and the target must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned for 1 minute. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 5 (2d4) Necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Explosive Skull (Recharge 5-6). The wither launches a flaming blue skull that explodes in a 10-foot-radius Sphere of necrotic energy at a point it can see within 90 feet of it. Each creature within that sphere must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or take 45 (13d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. On a failed save, the target is also poisoned until the end of its next turn. While poisoned in this way, the target takes 5 (2d4) necrotic damage at the end of each of its turns.


Reactions

Grim Harvest. When the wither kills a creature with necrotic damage the wither gains 5 (2d4) hit points.


Legendary Actions

The wither can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wither regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Withering Skull. The wither makes one withering skull attack.

Bed of Decay (Costs 2 Actions). A bed of decaying flowers sprout in a 10-foot-radius centered on a point the wither can see within 90 feet of it. Each creature within that area must succeed on a DC 19 Constitution saving throw or take 14 (4d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
