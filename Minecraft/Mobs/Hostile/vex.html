Hideous Laughter The vex magically targets a creature it can see within 30 feet of it. The creature must succeed on a DC 11 Wisdom saving throw or falls into fits of laughter. The creature becomes incapacitated for 1 minute. During that time, the creature laughs uncontrollably if it is capable of laughter. The creature repeats the save at the end of each of its turns and whenever it takes damage, ending the effect on itself on a success. The creature rolls with advantage if the save is triggered by damage.
