Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Technoblade's fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Weapons. Technoblade's weapon attacks are magical.


Second Wind (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). As a bonus action, Technoblade regains 20 hit points.


Thorns Armor. When technoblade is subjected to damage from an attack, the attacker takes 5 (2d4) Force damage.


Totem of Undying (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If Technoblade is reduced to 0 hit points, he doesn't die or become unconscious. Instead, his totem of undying activates, and he regains 149 hit points. In addition, the totem grants Technoblade 50 temporary hit points and immunity to fire damage for 1 hour. Additionally, Technoblade can now use the options in the "Mythic Actions" section for 1 hour. Award a party an additional 25,000 XP (50,000 XP total) for defeating Technoblade after his Totem of Undying activates.


Actions

Multiattack. Technoblade makes three attacks: two with his obliterator sword and one with his trident.


Obliterator Sword. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d8 + 5) slashing damage. If the target is a creature or a flammable object, it ignites. Until a creature takes an action to douse the fire, the target takes 5 (1d10) fire damage at the start of each of its turns.


Trident. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 12 (2d6 + 5) piercing damage, or 14 (2d8 + 5) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack.


Rocket Launcher (Recharge 5-6). Technoblade fires 3 rocket-powered arrows in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that cone must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw or take 56 (16d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. A creature that fails this saving throw by 5 or more is also blinded until the start of their next turn.


Reactions

Parry. Technoblade adds 5 to its AC against one melee attack that would hit it. To do so, Technoblade must see the attacker and be wielding a melee weapon.


Legendary Actions

Technoblade can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. He can take only one legendary action at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Technoblade regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Move. Technoblade moves up to his speed without provoking oportunity attacks.

Strike. Technoblade makes one obliterator sword attack.

Rocket Launcher (Costs 2 Actions). Technoblade uses Rocket Launcher.


Mythic Actions

If Technoblade's Totem of Undying trait has activated in the last hour, he can use the options below as legendary actions.


Slash. The Technoblade makes two obliterator sword attacks.

Block of TNT (Costs 2 Actions). Technoblade lauches a block of TNT into a 5-foot-square space within 60 feet of him. Each creature in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on that point must make a DC 19 Dexterity saving throw, taking 35 (10d6) bludgeoning damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one.
