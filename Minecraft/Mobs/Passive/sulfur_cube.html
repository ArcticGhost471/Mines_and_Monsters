Condition Immunities charmed, deafened, exhaustion, frightened, prone

Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 6

Languages —

Challenge 1/4 (50 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Docile Trance. When the cube has engulfed a creature or object, it enters a trance-like state. While in this trance, the cube's speed becomes 0, it can only be moved by other creatures, and can't use actions, bonus actions, or reactions. In addition, the cube becomes immune to all forms of damage except fire, force, poison, and psychic damage.


Ooze Cube. The cube takes up its entire space. Other creatures can enter the space, but a creature that does so is subjected to the cube's Engulf and has disadvantage on the saving throw.

 Creatures inside the cube can be seen but have total cover.

 A creature within 5 feet of the cube can take an action to pull a creature or object out of the cube. Doing so requires a successful DC 12 Strength check, and the creature making the attempt takes 2 (1d4) acid damage.

 The cube can hold only one creature of its size or smaller if it is Medium or smaller.


Property Absorption. When the cube has engulfed an object of its size or smaller, it gains certain properties based on the object within itself. When the cube is pushed or thrown, it has one of the following effects:

  1. Desecrated. The cube feels cursed and is unmoving. A creature can use an action make a DC 30 Strength check, moving the cube up to 10 feet on a success.
  2. Fungi. If the cube is on the ground, it moves up to the creature's Strength score every turn until it hits a surface or creature.
  3. Honey and Slime. The cube becomes sticky and can stick to flat surfaces.
  4. Ice. If the cube is on the ground, it moves up to twice the creature's Strength score every turn until it hits a surface or creature.
  5. Metals and Minerals. The cube's weight magically becomes 30 pounds. When falling, its fall speed is now double.
  6. Vegetable Matter. The cube rolls when it moves. When rolling downhill, it's rolling Speed equals to twice it's Speed.
  7. Wood. The cube bounces off any surface it comes into contact with. The bounce distance in any direction equals the creature's Strength score.
  8. Wool. The cube's weight magically becomes as light as a feather. When falling, it falls as if it were under the effects of the feather fall spell.
  9. Other. The cube bounces off surfaces it comes into contact with. The bounce distance in any direction equals half the creature's Strength score.

Actions

Pseudopod. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage.


Engulf. The cube moves up to its speed. While doing so, it can enter Medium or smaller creatures' or objects' spaces. Whenever the cube enters a creatures' space, the creature must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw. An object automatically fails the saving throw.

 On a successful save, the creature can choose to be pushed 5 feet back or to the side of the cube. A creature that chooses not to be pushed suffers the consequences of a failed saving throw.

 On a failed save, the cube enters the target's space, and the creature takes 2 (1d4) acid damage and is engulfed. An engulfed target can't breathe and is restrained. When the cube moves, the engulfed target moves with it.

 An engulfed target can try to escape by taking an action to make a DC 12 Strength check. On a successful check, the creature escapes and enters a space of its choice within 5 feet of the cube.


Reactions

Split. When a cube that is Medium is subjected to lightning or slashing damage, it splits into two new cubes if it has at least 10 hit points. Each new cube has hit points equal to half the original cube's, rounded down. New cube are one size smaller than the original cube.

Actions

Slam. Melee Attack Roll: +3, reach 5 ft. Hit: 4 (1d6 + 1) Acid damage.


Engulf. The cube moves up to its Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks. The cube can move through the spaces of Medium or smaller creatures or objects if it has room inside itself to contain them (see the Ooze Cube trait). Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 12, each creature or object whose space the cube enters for the first time during this move. Failure: 2 (1d4) Acid damage, and the target is engulfed. An engulfed target is suffocating, can't cast spells with a Verbal component, has the Restrained condition, and takes 2 (1d4) Acid damage at the start of each of the cube's turns. When the cube moves, the engulfed target moves with it. An engulfed target can try to escape by taking an action to make a DC 12 Strength (Athletics) check. On a successful check, the target escapes and enters the nearest unoccupied space. Success: Half damage, and the target moves to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the cube. If there is no unoccupied space, the target fails the save instead.


Reactions

Split. Trigger: While the cube is Medium and has 10+ Hit Points, it becomes Bloodied or is subjected to Lightning or Slashing damage. Response: The cube splits into two new Sulfur Cubes. Each new cube is one size smaller than the original cube and acts on its own initiative. The original cube's Hit Points are divided evenly between the new cubes (round down).
