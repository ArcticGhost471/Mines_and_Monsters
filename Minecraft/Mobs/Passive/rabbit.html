Variant: Killer Rabbit

Some rabbits are unlike other rabbits. Killer rabbits are creatures who pretend to be normal rabbits in order to lure their prey. These beasts are ruthless and bloodthirsty and will stop at nothing to get a meal.

 A killer rabbit is a chaotic evil monstrosity with a challenge rating of 2 (450 XP) and has a Strength of 18 (+4), as well as the following additional trait.


 Aggressive. As a bonus action, the killer rabbit can move up to its speed toward a hostile creature that it can see.


A killer rabbit also has the following additional action.


 Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (3d6 + 4) piercing damage.
