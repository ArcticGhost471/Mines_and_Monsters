Lava Walker. The strider can walk on lava as if it were harmless solid ground.


Water Susceptibility. The strider takes 1 cold damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 12). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the strider can't bite another target.


Swallow. The strider makes one bite attack against a Medium or smaller target it is grappling. If the attack hits, the target is swallowed, and the grapple ends. The swallowed target is blinded and restrained, it has total cover against attacks and other effects outside the strider, and it takes 10 (4d4) acid damage at the start of each of the strider's turns. The strider can have only one target swallowed at a time.

 If the strider dies, a swallowed creature is no longer restrained by it and can escape from the corpse using 5 feet of movement, exiting prone.
