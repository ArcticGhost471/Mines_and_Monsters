Spellcasting. The illager is a 10th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). The illager has the following wizard spells prepared:


Cantrips (at will): dancing lights, mage hand, message, minor illusion, prestidigitation

1st level (4 slots): detect magic, disguise self, illusory script, mage armor*

2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, mirror image

3rd level (3 slots): counterspell, major image, phantom steed

4th level (3 slots): greater invisibility, phantasmal killer

5th level (2 slots): creation, mislead


*The illager casts these spells on itself before combat


Actions

Shortbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.


Explosive Arrow (Recharge 5-6). The illager fires a firework strapped arrow at a point it can see within 120 feet. The arrow then explodes in a 15-foot-radius sphere centered on that point. Each creature in that sphere must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 31 (9d6) fire damage on a failed save or half as much on a successful one.


Mass Mirror (1/day). The illager vanishes in a cloud of smoke reappearing alongside 11 illusory duplicates in unoccupied spaces of its choice within 60 feet of its original position. The duplicates are indistinguishable from the illager and move with it, mimicking its actions. When a creature makes an attack roll against the illager or targets it with spell that requires a saving throw against the illager, roll a d12 to determine whether the attack hits the real illager or a duplicate. With 11 duplicates remaining, a roll of 11 or lower targets a duplicate. This threshold decreases by 1 for each destroyed duplicate. On a roll higher than the current threshold, the attack targets the real illager instead. A duplicate is destroyed if it is hit by an attack or fails their saving throws (duplicates automatically fail saving throws) The effect ends if the illager dismisses, is hit by an attack, or fails a saving throw against a damaging spell or effect. When the effect ends, all remaining duplicates vanish.
