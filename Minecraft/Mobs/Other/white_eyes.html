Detect Life. White eyes can magically sense the presence of living creatures up to 5 miles away. She knows the general direction they're in but not their exact locations.


Innate Spellcasting (3/day). White eyes can innately cast minor illusion (spell save DC 19), requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom.


Shadow Stealth. While in dim light or darkness, white eyes can take the Hide action as a bonus action.


Spider Climb. White eyes can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.


Sunlight Weakness. While in sunlight, white eyes has disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws.


Tunneler. White eyes can burrow through solid rock at half her burrowing speed and leaves a 10-foot-diameter tunnel in her wake.


Web Walker. White eyes ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.


Actions

Multiattack. White eyes makes three foreleg attacks.


Foreleg. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage.


Eerie Ambiance. Each creature of white eyes' choice that is within 120 feet of her must succeed on a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, with disadvantage if white eyes is within line of sight, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target's saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to white eyes' Eerie Ambience for the next 24 hours.


Teleport. White eyes magically teleports, along with any equipment she is wearing or carrying, up to 120 feet to an unoccupied space she can see.


Legendary Actions

White eyes can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. She regains spent legendary actions at the start of her turn.


Foreleg. White eyes makes one foreleg attack.

Move. White eyes moves up to her speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Shriek (Costs 2 Actions). White eyes unleashes a horrid shriek. Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw, taking 14 (4d6) thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Shriek. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 19, each creature in a 30-foot Cone. Failure: 14 (4d6) Thunder damage, and the target has the Frightened condition until the end of its next turn. Failure or Success: White eyes can't take this action again until the start of her next turn.
