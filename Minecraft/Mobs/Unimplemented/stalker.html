Echolocation. The stalker can't use its blindsight while deafened.


False Appearance. While the stalker remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal treasure totem.


Keen Hearing. The stalker has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the stalker fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The stalker has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Shapechanger. The stalker can use its action to distort its body to resemble a Medium wolf. While in this form, its walking speed is 40 feet, and it can use a bonus action to contort its body further to fit in Small places, reverting at the end of its turn. It takes 5 (1d10) Bludgeoning damage if it ends its turn inside a Small space. Its statistics, other than its size and speed, are unchanged. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. It reverts to its true form if it dies, unless it is in a space for a Medium or smaller creature.


Actions

Multiattack (Totem Form Only). The stalker makes two attacks, only one of which can be a bite attack.


Slam (Totem Form Only). Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 5 (2d4) necrotic damage.


Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage.


Legendary Actions

The stalker can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The stalker regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Detect. The stalker makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

Move. The stalker moves up to its speed.

Slam. The stalker makes one slam attack.

Bonus Actions

Shape-Shift. The stalker distorts its body to resemble a Medium wolf (Speed 40 ft.), or it returns to its true totem form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. Its game statistics, other than its size and Speed, are unchanged.


Contort (Wolf Form Only). The stalker contorts its body to fit into Small spaces, reverting at the end of its turn. It takes 5 (1d10) Bludgeoning damage if it ends its turn inside a Small space.


Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3. Immediately after another creature's turn, The stalker can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The stalker regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.


Corroding Rust. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 16, one creature within 60 feet of the stalker. Failure: 10 (3d6) Necrotic damage, and if the target is a creature, it is cursed. Until the curse ends, the target's skin becomes corroded, it can't regain Hit Points, and takes 6 (1d12) Necrotic damage at the end of every hour. A creature dies and turns to rust if reduced to 0 Hit Points by this attack. Success: Half damage only. Failure or Success: The stalker can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Distorted Strike. The stalker moves up to half its Speed, and it makes one Slam attack.
