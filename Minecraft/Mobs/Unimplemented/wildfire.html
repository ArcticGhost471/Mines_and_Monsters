Elemental Shields. The wildfire is protected by 4 plates of fire, gaining a +8 bonus to its AC (included in its AC). Each plate is a Large object and has AC 10; 24 hit points; vulnerable to cold damage; resistant to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing; and immunity to fire, poison, and psychic damage. If a plate is destroyed, the wildfire's AC decreases by 2.


Fire Absorption. Whenever the wildfire is subjected to fire damage, it takes no damage and instead regains a number of hit points equal to the fire damage dealt.


Heat Aura. Any creature that starts its turn within 5 feet of the wildfire takes 7 (2d6) fire damage.


Illumination. The wildfire sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the wildfire fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Water Susceptibility. The wildfire takes 1 cold damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Multiattack. The wildfire makes three fiery debris attacks.


Fiery Debris. Ranged Spell Attack: +9 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (3d6 + 4) fire damage, and the target catches fire it its a creature or a flammable object. Until a creature takes an action to extinguish the fire, the burning target takes 7 (2d6) fire damage at the end of each of its turns.


Flames of the Nether (1/day). The wildfire magically summons 1 or 2 blazes. The summoned blazes appear in unoccupied spaces within 30 feet of the wildfire, whom they obey. They take their turns immediately after the wildfire. Each blaze lasts for 1 hour, until it or the wildfire dies, or until the wildfire dismisses it as a bonus action.


Legendary Actions

The wildfire can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wildfire regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Move. The wildfire moves up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

Retaliatory Shockwave (Costs 2 Actions). The wildfire causes a small shockwave within 10 feet of itself. Each creature in that area must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the target takes 22 (5d8) thunder damage and is pushed up to 10 feet straight away from the wildfire. On a successful save, the target takes half the thunder damage and isn't pushed back or knocked prone.

Restore Shield (Costs 3 Actions). The wildfire uses its magic to restore one of its plates of fire if at least one of them is destroyed. The Wildfire regains a plate and gains a +2 bonus to AC until the start of its next turn, upon which the new plate vanishes.
