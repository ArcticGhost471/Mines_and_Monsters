Contract Bound. The piglin is magically bound to a contract held by another creature. As long as the piglin and its contract are on the same plane of existance, the contract holder can telepathically call the piglin to travel to it, and the piglin knows the distance and direction to the holder. If the piglin is within 60 feet of the contract holder, half of any damage the holder takes (rounded up) is transferred to the piglin. As long as the contract remains active, the Zombification Plague trait doesn't affect the piglin. If the contract is broken—such as through dismissal, betrayal, or abandonment—while the piglin is outside the Lower Planes, the transformation into a zombified piglin occurs immediately. If the contract is fulfilled or otherwise ends under mutually agreed terms, the Zombification Plague resumes normally.


Pack Tactics. The piglin has advantage on attack rolls against a creature if at least one of the piglin's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't incapacitated.


Zombification Plague. If the piglin remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, its body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiattack. The piglin makes two weapon attacks.


Golden Sword. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (2d6 + 3) slashing damage.


Light Crossbow. Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.


Reactions

Shield. When a creature makes an attack against the piglin's contract holder, the piglin grants a +2 bonus to the wearer's AC if the piglin is within 5 feet of the wearer.

Protection. Trigger: An attack roll hits the piglin's contract holder while the holder is within 5 feet of the piglin. Response: The contract holder gains a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack and possibly causing it to miss, until the start of the piglin's next turn.
