Innate Spellcasting. Malgosha's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 22). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: detect magic, dispel magic, witch bolt

3/day: blight, counterspell, crown of madness, darkness, synaptic static

1/day: feeblemind


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Malgosha fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. Malgosha has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. Malgosha's weapon attacks are magical.


Orb of Dominance. The orb has 7 charges, and any of its properties that require a saving throw have a save DC of 18. While holding it, Malgosha can use an action to cast detect thoughts, dissonant whispers, or speak with dead. Alternatively, she can expend 1 or more of the orb's charges to cast one of the following spells from it: disintegrate (1 charge), telekinesis (1 charge), or dominate monster (2 charges). The orb regains 1d4 + 3 charges daily at dawn.


Zombification Plague. If Malgosha remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, her body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiattack. Malgosha makes three dagger attacks.


Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d4 + 6) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) psychic damage.


Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage, or 15 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage.


Legendary Actions

Malgosha can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Malgosha regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Elder Blast. Malgosha casts witch bolt.

Secret Dagger. Malgosha makes one dagger attack.

Corrupted Beacon (Cost 2 Actions). Malgosha holds up the Orb of Dominance and fires a 120-feet line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 22 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
