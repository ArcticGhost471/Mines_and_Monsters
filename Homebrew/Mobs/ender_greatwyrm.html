End Awakening (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If the greatwyrm would be reduced to 0 hit points, its current hit point total instead resets to 450 hit points, it recharges its Breath Weapon, and it regains any expended uses of Legendary Resistance. Additionally, the greatwyrm can now use the options in the "Mythic Actions" section for 1 hour. Award a party an additional 120,000 XP (240,000 XP total) for defeating the greatwyrm after its End Awakening activates.


Legendary Resistance (4/day). If the greatwyrm fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Actions

Multiattack. The greatwyrm makes one Bite attack and two Claw attacks.


Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (2d10 + 10) piercing damage plus 13 (2d12) force damage.


Claw. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (2d6 + 10) slashing damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 20) and is restrained until this grapple ends. The greatwyrm can have only one creature grappled in this way at a time.


Tail. Melee Weapon Attack: +16 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 19 (2d8 + 10) bludgeoning damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 26 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.


Harmful Breath (Recharge 5-6). The greatwyrm exhales putrid gas in a 300-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 26 Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes 78 (12d12) necrotic damage. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage. The greatwyrm then chooses a point it can see where the cone makes contact with the ground. That point becomes the center of a 90-foot-high, 90-foot-radius cylinder of lingering gas that disappears at the start of the greatwyrm's next turn. Any creature that starts its turn in the cylinder takes 38 (11d6) necrotic damage.


Legendary Actions

The greatwyrm can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The greatwyrm regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Attack. The greatwyrm makes one Claw or Tail attack.

Wing Attack (Costs 2 Actions). The greatwyrm beats its wings. Each creature within 30 feet of the greatwyrm must succeed on a DC 26 Dexterity saving throw or take 17 (2d6 + 10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. The greatwyrm can then fly up to half its flying speed.

Voidfire Ball (Costs 3 Actions). The greatwyrm unleashes an ball of void-tainted necrotic energy that detonates in a 60-foot-radius sphere at a point it can see within 240 feet of it. Each creature within that sphere must make a DC 26 Constitution saving throw, taking 49 (14d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Mythic Actions

If the greatwyrm's End Awakening trait has activated in the last hour, it can use the options below as legendary actions.


Bite. The greatwyrm makes one Bite attack.

Astral Surge (Costs 2 Actions). The greatwyrm surges with astral energy. Each creature in a 120-foot-radius Sphere centered on the greatwyrm must succeed on a DC 26 Strength saving throw or take 19 (3d12) force damage and be pushed up to 40 feet away from the greatwyrm.

Actions

Multiattack. The greatwyrm makes three rend attacks. It can replace one attack with a use of Spellcasting to cast Magic Missile (level 4 version).


Rend. Melee Attack Roll: +16, reach 15 ft. Hit: 19 (2d8 + 10) Slashing damage plus 14 (4d6) Necrotic damage.


Harmful Breath (Recharge 5-6). Constitution Saving Throw: DC 26, each creature in a 90-foot Cone. Failure: 78 (12d12) Necrotic damage. Success: Half Damage. Failure or Success: The greatwyrm chooses a point it can see where the cone makes contact with the ground. That point becomes the center of a 20-foot-high, 20-foot-radius cylinder of lingering gas that disappears at the start of the greatwyrm's next turn. Any creature that starts its turn in the cylinder takes 14 (4d6) Necrotic damage.


Spellcasting. The greatwyrm casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 24):


At will: Detect Magic, Magic Missile (level 4 version), Suggestion

1/day each: Circle of Death (level 8 version), Dominate Monster, Meteor Swarm, Scrying, Teleport


Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3. Immediately after another creature's turn, The greatwyrm can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The greatwyrm regains all expended uses at the start of each of its turns.


Astral Surge. Strength Saving Throw: DC 26, each creature in a 60-foot Emmanation originating from the greatwyrm. Failure: 26 (4d12) force damage, and the target, if it is Medium or smaller, is pushed up to 40 feet away from the greatwyrm and has the Prone condition. Failure or Success: The greatwyrm can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Pounce. The greatwyrm moves up to half its Speed, and it makes one Rend attack.


Voidfire Ball. Constitution Saving Throw: DC 26, each creature in a 30-foot-radius Sphere centered on a point the greatwyrm can see within 120 feet. Failure: 45 (13d6) Necrotic damage, and the target has the Poisoned condition until the end of its next turn. Failure or Success: The greatwyrm can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.
