Implosion. When the farlander drops to 0 hit points, it dies as its body implodes.


Innate Spellcasting. The farlander's innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 14). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: Dancing Lights, Druidcraft

3/day: Faerie Fire, Fog Cloud

1/day each: Detect Thoughts, Fear


Magic Resistance. The farlander has Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the farlander has disadvantage on attack rolls.


Water Susceptibility. The farlander takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Multiattack. The farlander makes two Slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.


Teleport. The farlander magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
