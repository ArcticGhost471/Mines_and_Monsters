Horde. The horde can share its space with other creatures, and enemies treat the space as difficult terrain. It can move through any opening large enough for a Medium creature.

 The horde can take one reaction every creature's turn in combat. No effect (positive or negative) functions on the horde unless it applies to at least half of the horde's space, except for damage and healing which function normally.


Zombification Plague. If the horde remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, its body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiatttack. The horde makes two weapon attacks, or one weapon attack if the horde has half of its hit points or fewer.


Maces. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.


Blaze Rods. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 30/120 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d6 + 4) fire damage.
