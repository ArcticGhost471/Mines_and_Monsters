Charge. If The Beast moves at least 20 feet straight toward a target and then hits it with a gore attack on the same turn, the target takes an extra 16 (3d10) piercing damage. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.


Innate Spellcasting. The Beast's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 16). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: detect magic

3/day: dominate beast, hunter's mark


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If The Beast fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Beast has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The Beast's weapon attacks are magical.


Reckless. At the start of his turn, The Beast can gain advantage on all melee weapon attack rolls during that turn, but attack rolls against him have advantage until the start of his next turn.


Zombification Plague. If The Beast remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, his body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiattack. The Beast makes three attacks: two with his greatclub and one with his gore attack.


Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (2d6 + 7) piercing damage.


Greatclub. Melee Weapon Attack: +13 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (2d8 + 7) bludgeoning damage.


Horde of the Hunt (1/day). The Beast calls upon a Piglin Horde. The horde arrives in 1d4 rounds, acting as an ally of the beast and obeying its spoken commands. The horde remains for 1 hour, until the beast dies, or until the beast dismisses them as a bonus action.


Legendary Actions

The Beast can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Beast regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Greatclub. The Beast makes one greatclub attack.

Charge (Cost 2 Actions). The Beast moves up to his speed, then makes a gore attack.


The Beast's Lair

The Beast's lair is an encampment set on the first layer of Gehenna, Khalas, known as the Hunting Grounds. Surrounded by walls of bone, what the lair lacks in defenses, it makes up in size and numbers. The Hunting Ground's less fortified nature makes it the perfect environment for the beast to hunt. As The Beast chooses to not linger on a spot for too long, the Hunting Grounds has been seen in many places in Khalas and he and his horde of piglins, warboars, and pigmadillos revel in the nomadic lifestyle.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), The Beast takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; The Beast can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Beastly Charge. The Beast rears up, moves up to its speed, and rams through a wall of its lair within range, destroying it. The wall remains destroyed until The Beast uses this lair action again on another wall, in which case a group of piglins will rush in to rebuild the former.
  • Hoglin Stampede. A herd of hoglins rush through the battlefield, charging into creatures in a 60-foot line that is 10-feet wide at a point The Beast can see within 120 feet of itself. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 10 (3d6) piercing damage.
  • Hunting Call. A war horn is heard somewhere within the beast's lair upon which The Beast responds with a war cry of his own. Each creature of The Beast's choice that it can see within 60 feet must succeed on a DC 15 Charisma saving throw or become frightened until initiative count 20 on the next round.

Regional Effect

The region containing The Beast's lair is warped by his magic, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Arid Wind. Dry wind blows within 1 mile of the lair.
  • Nether Wastes. The land within 1 mile of the lair takes twice as long as normal to traverse, since nether grass grows long and dry and tar pits are thick and plentiful.
  • Panicked Beasts. Beasts within 1 mile of the lair become frightened and disoriented, as though constantly under threat of being hunted, and might lash out or panic even when no visible threat is nearby.

If The Beast dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.

Bone Club. The Beast makes one Bone Club attack.

The Beast's Lair

The Beast's lair is an encampment, known as the Hunting Grounds, that is found on the first layer of Gehenna, Khalas.


Regional Effects

The region containing The Beast's lair is warped by his magic, creating the following effects:


Nether Haze. Dry wind blows within 1 mile of the lair and nether grass grow tall and dry, rendering that area Lightly Obscured. Travel for creatures other than The Beast and his allies takes twice the usual time in that area.

Panicked Beasts. Beasts within 1 mile of the lair have the Frightened condition and become disoriented, as though constantly under threat of being hunted, and might lash out or panic even when no visible threat is nearby.


If The Beast dies or moves his lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
