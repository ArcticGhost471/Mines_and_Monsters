Innate Spellcasting. The Unbreakable's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 22). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: command, detect magic, firebolt (17th level)

3/day: blade barrier, fear, fireball (5th level), heat metal

1/day: circle of power, earthquake


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If The Unbreakable fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Unbreakable has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The Unbreakable's weapon attacks are magical.


Relentless (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). If the Unbreakable takes 42 damage or less that would reduce it to 0 hit points, it is reduced to 1 hit point instead.


Zombification Plague. If The Unbreakable remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, his body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiattack. The Unbreakable makes one slam attack and two magma cannon attack.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +15 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage.


Magma Cannon. Ranged Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (3d10 + 2) fire damage.


Flamethrower (Recharge 5-6). The Unbreakable expels fire from its arm cannon in a 60-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw, taking 72 (16d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.


Cube Rain (1/day). The Unbreakable causes 1d4 magma cubes to appear in unoccupied spaces within 15 feet of it. The cubes act as allies of the Unbreakable and remain for 1 hour or until the Unbreakable dies.


Legendary Actions

The Unbreakable can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Unbreakable regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Attack. The Unbreakable makes one slam attack.

Magma Shot. The Unbreakable uses magma shot.

Furnace Backlog (Cost 2 Actions). The Unbreakable spews lava from its furnace generator in a 30-foot cone behind him. Each creature in that cone must succeed DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or take 16 (3d10) fire damage.



The Unbreakable's Lair

The Unbreakable's lair is a mighty fortress, the Golden Bastion, which is on the second layer of Gehenna, Chamada. Among the raging furnace of Chamada, the Golden Bastion is painstaikingly maintained and fortified by his followers and surrounded by a moat of lava. The structure is defended by walls and walls of defenses, traps, and is almost labyrinthine in structure; it is populated by piglins, bruisers, and portal guards.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), The Unbreakable takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; The Unbreakable can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Close the Gates. The Unbreakable raises gates on one doorway or other entryway within the lair. The opening must be unoccupied. It is blocked with magical iron gates for 1 minute or until the Unbreakable creates this effect again.
  • Lava Coil. A lava coil in the lair emits a magical pulse. The Unbreakable and any allies within 30 feet of it gain 30 temporary hit points and gain a +2 bonus to AC.
  • Magma Geyser. Magma erupts from a point on the ground the Unbreakable can see within 120 feet of it, creating a 20-foot-high, 5-foot-radius geyser. Each creature in the geyser's area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Regional Effect

The region containing The Unbreakable's lair is warped by his magic, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Crimson Spores. Fungal spores make the air within 6 miles of the lair tolerable for non-natives of Chamada.
  • Lava Flow. Lava flow within 1 mile of the lair is unnaturally calm, yet lava within the lair's moats rage twice as fierce.
  • Molten Allies. Magma Cubes and Blazes within 1 mile of the lair become charmed and gain the ability to understand Piglin.

If The Unbreakable dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.
