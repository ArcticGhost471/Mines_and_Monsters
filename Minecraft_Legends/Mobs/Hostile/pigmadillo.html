Piglin Restoration. If the pigmadillo dies outside Gehenna, its body dissolves into red mist, leaves behind a porkchop, and it gains a new body instantly, reviving with all its Hit Points somewhere in Gehenna. A pigmadillo that has transformed due to the Zombification Plague cannot return in this way.
