Celestial Weapons. Knowledge's weapon attacks are magical. When knowledge hits with any weapon, the weapon deals an extra 13 (3d8) radiant damage (included in the attack).


Innate Spellcasting. Knowledge's spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 24). He can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: detect evil and good, magic weapon, sending

3/day each: creation, fabricate

1/day each: animate objects, gate


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Knowledge fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. Knowledge has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Actions

Multiattack. Knowledge makes three attacks: two with his shortsword and one with his battleaxe. Alternatively, he can make two flame of creation attacks.


Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage plus 13 (3d8) radiant damage.


Battleaxe. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d8 + 4) slashing damage plus 13 (3d8) radiant damage.


Flame of Creation. Ranged Spell Attack: +16 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 37 (6d8 + 10) radiant damage.


Etherealness. Knowledge enters the Ethereal Plane from the Material Plane, or vice versa. He is visible on the Material Plane while he is in the Border Ethereal, and vice versa, yet he can't affect or be affected by anything on the other plane.


Bonus Actions

Defenders of Fate (1/day). Knowledge magically summons 5 cobblestone, grindstone, or plank golems. The summoned creatures appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of Knowledge, whom they obey. They take their turns immediately after Knowledge. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or Knowledge dies, or until Knowledge dismisses it as a bonus action.


Legendary Actions

Knowledge can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Knowledge regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Ethereal Form. Knowledge uses Etherealness.

Smite. Knowledge makes one shortsword, battleaxe, or flame of creation attack.

Legendary Lute (Costs 2 Actions). Knowledge plays a soothing melody on his lute and targets a creature he can see within 60 feet. That creature must succeed on a DC 24 Charisma saving throw or become charmed for 1 minute. The creature can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.


The Host's Lair

The Hosts take residence in the Well of Fate, a celestial settlement located in the Ethereal Plane. It exists in the Border Ethereal where the hosts can peer into the Material Plane without interfering in the affairs of mortals.


Regional Effect

The region containing the Host's lair is warped by its magic, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Anomalous Existance. The lair has a physical presence on both the Material Plane and the Border Ethereal at once, appearing as an elaborate temple in the Material Plane. A creature that uses the etherealness spell while at the temple is transported into the lair in the Border Ethereal.
  • Ethereal Presence. Creatures not native to the Ethereal Plane have a translucent appearance while in the lair.
  • Portals of Fate. Once per day, the Host can open a portal linking to another plane of existence. They don't need to know the name of a specific creature, otherwise the effect is identical to the gate spell.

If the Host dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.
