Antimagic Susceptibility. The golem is incapacitated while in the area of an antimagic field. If targetted by dispel magic, the golem must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against the caster's spell save DC or fall unconscious for 1 minute.


Gateway to the Nether. The golem's body contains a purple 10-foot-tall gateway that is 5 feet wide to the plane of Gehenna. If the golem is in Gehenna, the gateway instead leads to the Material Plane. A creature can use this gateway to travel from the current plane to Gehenna or the Material Plane and viceversa unless the golem is incapacitated or drops to 0 hit points in which case the gateway closes until reopened. A creature can use an action to reopen the gateway by lighting a small flame in the golem's body.


Immutable Form. The golem is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Magic Resistance. The golem has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The golem's weapon attacks are magical.


Actions

Multiattack. The golem makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage.
