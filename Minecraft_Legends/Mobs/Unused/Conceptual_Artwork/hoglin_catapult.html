Trampling Charge. If the hoglin moves at least 20 feet straight toward a creature and then hits it with a gore attack on the same turn, that target must succeed on a DC 17 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone. If the target is prone, the elephant can make one stomp attack against it as a bonus action.


Zombification Plague. If the hoglin remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, its body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified hoglin. A zombified hoglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.

Gore. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (3d8 + 5) piercing damage.


Stomp. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 5 ft., one prone creature. Hit: 21 (3d10 + 5) bludgeoning damage.


Catapult (Recharge 5-6). The hoglin fires its back mounted catapult, launching a boulder at a creature it can see within 120 feet of it. That creature must succeed on a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw or take 27 (5d10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.

Catapult (Recharge 5-6). Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 17, one creature the hoglin can see within 120 feet. Failure: 27 (5d10) Fire damage and if the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the Prone condition.
