Innate Spellcasting. The Seer's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 22). She can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: detect magic, dispel magic, witch bolt

3/day: blight, counterspell, crown of madness, darkness, synaptic static

1/day: feeblemind


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If The Seer fails a saving throw, she can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Seer has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The Seer's weapon attacks are magical.


Orb of Dominance. The orb has 7 charges, and any of its properties that require a saving throw have a save DC of 18. While holding it, the Seer can use an action to cast detect thoughts, dissonant whispers, or speak with dead. Alternatively, she can expend 1 or more of the orb's charges to cast one of the following spells from it: disintegrate (1 charge), telekinesis (1 charge), or dominate monster (2 charges). The orb regains 1d4 + 3 charges daily at dawn.

 The Orb of Dominance has AC 15, HP 20, and Resistance to all damage. The orb shatters if reduced to 0 Hit Points. If the orb is destroyed, any souls the orb has absorbed are released.


Zombification Plague. If The Seer remains outside the Lower Planes for 30 minutes without exposure to Nether Wart, her body quickly deteriorates into undead flesh and becomes a zombified piglin. A zombified piglin can die permanently outside of its native plane.


Actions

Multiattack. The Seer makes three dagger attacks.


Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d4 + 6) piercing damage plus 10 (3d6) psychic damage.


Quarterstaff. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage, or 15 (3d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) force damage.


Legendary Actions

The Seer can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Seer regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Elder Blast. The Seer casts witch bolt.

Secret Dagger. The Seer makes one dagger attack.

Corrupted Beacon (Cost 2 Actions). The Seer holds up the Orb of Dominance and fires a 120-feet line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 22 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.


The Seer's Lair

The Seer makes her lair in a tower called Dominion, found on the fourth layer of Gehenna, Krangath. The tall spiraling tower evokes feelings of unease and dread to those that lay eyes on it. It's structure feel both natural and out of place in Gehenna, as if pulled from a different plane while also having been created on the very plane it exists in. Some say you can hear the call of the Far Realm from within its walls.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), The Seer takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; The Seer can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Switch Places. The Seer chooses two room within the lair that is no larger in any dimension than 50 feet. Until initiative count 20, the contents of both rooms are swapped with one another. Any creatures or objects in one of the rooms are magically transported into the other room on spaces equivalent to their original positions.
  • Words of Dominance. The Seer utters a spell onto the Orb of Dominance and chooses up to three creature within the lair. Each creature must succeed on a DC 21 Wisdom saving throw or take 33 (6d10) Psychic damage. If the action kills the target, its soul is trapped in the orb, and the target can't be raised from the dead until its soul is released. The Orb of Dominance regains a charge per soul trapped. Destroying the orb or succeeding on a DC 21 Wisdom saving throw against it causes the souls to be released.

Regional Effect

The region containing The Seer's lair is warped by her magic, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Agnostophobia. A sense of unease fills the air within 6 miles of the lair. Any creature (excluding the Seer and her allies) that finishes a Short Rest in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or gain no benefit from that rest.
  • Discordant Whispers. Creatures within 1 mile of the lair can faintly hear whispers in their mind.
  • The Seer's Madness. If a humanoid spends at least 1 hour within 1 mile of the lair, that creature must succeed on a DC 21 Wisdom saving throw or descend into a madness determined by the Madness of the Seer table. A creature that succeeds on this saving throw can't be affected by this regional effect again for 24 hours.

If The Seer dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.


Madness of the Seer

If a creature goes mad in the Seer's lair or within line of sight of the piglin general, roll on the Madness of the Seer table to determine the nature of the madness, which is a character flaw that lasts until cured. See the Dungeon Master's Guide for more on madness.

d100 Flaw (lasts until cured)
