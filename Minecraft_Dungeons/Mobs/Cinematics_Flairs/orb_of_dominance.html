Cosmic Ressurection. When the Orb of Dominance drops to 0 hit points, its crystaline form then shatters and its divine spark vanishes. It is dead until one of the Orb of Dominance's shards is recovered and returned to the Astral Plane, where it reappears as The Orb of Dominance at full health in its imperfect form.


Divine Being. The Orb of Dominance can't be surprised and can't be changed into another form against its will.


Innate Spellcasting. The Orb of Dominance's spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 20). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:


At will: Detect Thoughts, Dissonant Whispers, Speak With Dead

2/day each: Disintegrate, Telekinesis

1/day each: Dominate Monster


Legendary Resistance (1/day). If the Orb of Dominance fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Orb of Dominance has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Actions

Multiattack. The Orb of Dominance makes three voidfire ray attacks.


Voidfire Ray. Ranged Spell Attack: +12 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 18 (4d8) necrotic damage.


Radiant Streak (Recharge 5-6). The orb expels searing light in a 60-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC 20 Constitution saving throw, taking 52 (15d6) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Legendary Actions

The Orb of Dominance can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Orb of Dominance regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Voidfire Ray. The Orb of Dominance make a voidfire ray attack.

Cacophonous Whispers (Costs 2 Actions) The Orb of Dominance causes a cacophonous chorus of whispers to ring in the mind of a creature it can see within 120 feet of it that isn't an Aberration. That creature must make a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw, taking 16 (3d10) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Teleport (Cost 2 Actions). The Orb of Dominance teleports to an unoccupied space it can see within 60 feet.


The Orb of Dominance's Lair

The Orb of Dominance may settle or be restricted to hard to reach, isolated locations. In such cases, it tempts travelers with its presence and leads them to releasing its power.


Regional Effect

The region containing The Orb of Dominance's lair is warped by its presence, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Call of Ender. Creatures within 6 miles of the lair are compelled to approach it.
  • Mental Grip. The Orb of Dominance can impose its will upon creatures within its lair, giving them a command to follow. A creature of the Orb of Dominance's choice in the lair must make a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or be charmed by the Orb of Dominance until it completes its given command.

If The Orb of Dominance dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.

Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the Orb of Dominance can expend a use to take one of the following actions. The Orb of Dominance regains all expended uses at the start of each of their turns.


Cacophonous Whispers. Wisdom Saving Throw: DC 20, one creature the Orb of Dominance can see within 120 feet. Failure: 22 (4d10) Psychic damage, and the target has the Stunned condition until the end of its next turn. Success: Half damage only. Failure or Success: The Orb of Dominance can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Entropic Beam. The Orb of Dominance makes one Voidfire Ray attack.


Hollow Teleport. The Orb of Dominance teleports up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see, and each creature within 10 feet of the space it left takes 11 (2d10) Necrotic damage.


The Orb of Dominance's Lair

The Orb of Dominance's may linger in mountains, abandoned castles, or other isolated locations.


Regional Effects

The region containing The Orb of Dominance's lair is warped by its presence, creating the following effects:


Call of Ender. Creatures within 1 mile of the lair feel a lingering sensation in the back of their minds. Any creature (excluding the Orb of Dominance and its allies) that finishes a Short Rest while within 1 mile of the lair must succeed on a DC 20 Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition for 1 hour. While charmed in this way, the creature is compelled to approach the lair.

Mental Grip. While in its lair, the Orb of Dominance can cast Dominate Person, requiring no Material components and using the same spellcasting ability as its Spellcasting action. When casting the spell this way, the spell's range is 1 mile, and the Orb of Dominance doesn't need to see the creature, only know its presence.


If The Orb of Dominance dies or moves its lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
