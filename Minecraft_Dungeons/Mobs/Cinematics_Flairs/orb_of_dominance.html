Cosmic Ressurection. When the Orb of Dominance drops to 0 hit points, its crystaline form then shatters and its divine spark vanishes. Divine Being. The Orb of Dominance can't be surprised and can't be changed into another form against its will.


Legendary Resistance (1/day). If the Orb of Dominance fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Orb of Dominance has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Actions

Multiattack. The Orb of Dominance makes two voidfire ray attacks.


Voidfire Ray. Ranged Spell Attack: +12 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d8) necrotic damage.


Radiant Outburst (Recharge 5-6). The orb expels searing light in a 30-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 19 Constitution saving throw, taking 28 (8d6) radiant damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Legendary Actions

The Orb of Dominance can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Orb of Dominance regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Voidfire Ray. The Orb of Dominance make a voidfire ray attack.

Cacophonous Whispers (Costs 2 Actions) The Orb of Dominance causes a cacophonous chorus of whispers to ring in the mind of a creature it can see within 120 feet of it that isn't an Aberration. That creature must make a DC 19 Wisdom saving throw, taking 16 (3d10) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

Teleport (Cost 2 Actions). The Orb of Dominance teleports to an unoccupied space it can see within 60 feet.
