Immutable Form. The monstrosity is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the monstrosity fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The monstrosity has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The monstrosity's weapon attacks are magical.


Siege Monster. The monstrosity deals double damage to objects and structures.


Actions

Multiattack. The monstrosity makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage.


Arcane Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d8) force damage.


Explosive Terrain (Recharge 5-6). The monstrosity magically summons purple mines in a 60-foot radius centered on itself. Each creature in that area must make a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw, taking 44 (8d10) Force damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Legendary Actions

The monstrosity can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The monstrosity regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Attack. The monstrosity makes one attack.

Move. The monstrosity moves up to half its speed.

Shockwave (Costs 2 Actions). The monstrosity stomps the ground, creating a shockwave that emmanates from itself. All creatures on the ground within 60 feet of the monstrosity must succeed on a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw or be knocked prone.
