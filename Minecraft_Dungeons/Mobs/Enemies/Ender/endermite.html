Variant: Endermite Familiar

Some endermites behave diferrently from others of its kind, acting a lot friendlier than what has come to be expected of an endermite. Sometimes dubbed Friendermites, these endermites often imprint themselves onto other creatures, willingly traveling alongside them as pets and companions. They are extremely fond of spellcasters, willingly becoming life-long partners as their alien familiar. Such endermites have the following trait.

 Familiar. The endermite can serve another creature as a familiar, forming a telepathic bond with its willing friend. While the two are bonded, the friend can sense what the endermite senses as long as they are within 1 mile of each other. At any time and for any reason, the endermite can end its service as a familiar, ending the telepathic bond.
