Legendary Resistance (3/day). If the false king fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The false king has advantage on saving throws against spells and magical effects.


Innate Spellcasting. The false king's innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 11, +8 to hit with spell attacks). It can innately cast the following spells, requiring no spell components:


At will: Detect Magic, Ice Knife, Mage Hand, Prestidigitation

2/day each: Dispel Magic, Nondetection

1/day each: Dimension Door, Sunbeam


Actions

Death Rend. Melee Spell Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 10 (3d6) necrotic damage.


Bloodcurdling Lament (Recharge 5-6). The false king emits a hideous shriek charged with malignant energy. Each creature within 30 feet of the false king must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or have the frightened condition for 1 minute. While frightened in this way, a creature also has the unconscious condition. An affected creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.


Legendary Actions

The false king can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The false king regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Spiteful Teleport. The false king, along with anything it is wearing or carrying, teleports to an unoccupied space it can see within 60 feet of itself. It then makes one Death Rend attack if possible.

Cast a Spell (Costs 2 Actions). The false king uses Spellcasting.
