Fire Trail. When the skeleton moves, it leaves behind a 5-foot-wide line of fire in its path that disappears at the start of its next turn. Each creature that moves through the line must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Gravity Pulse. At the start of the skeleton's turn, the skeleton emits a gravitational pulse within 30 feet of itself. Each non-allied creature must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 5 feet straight towards the skeleton.


Spellcasting. The skeleton is a 12th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). The skeleton has the following wizard spells prepared:


Cantrips (at will): chill touch, mage hand, mending, message, prestidigitation

1st level (4 slots): detect magic, false life, mage armor, ray of sickness

2nd level (3 slots): blindness/deafness, phantasmal force, ray of enfeeblement

3rd level (3 slots): animate dead, counterspell, vampiric touch

4th level (3 slots): blight, dimension door

5th level (2 slots): Bigby's hand, cloudkill

6th level (1 slots): circle of death


Actions

Multiattack. The skeleton makes three Arcane Burst attacks.


Arcane Burst. Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) necrotic damage.


Bonus Actions

Summon Zombies (1/day). The skeleton magically summons five zombies. The summoned zombies appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the skeleton, whom they obey. They take their turns immediately after skeleton. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or the skeleton dies, or until the skeleton dismisses it as a bonus action.
