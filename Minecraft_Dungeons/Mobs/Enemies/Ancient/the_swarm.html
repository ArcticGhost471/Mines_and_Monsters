Challenge 3 (700 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Gravity Pulse. At the start of the snareling's turn, the snareling emits a gravitational pulse within 30 feet of itself. Each non-allied creature must succeed on a DC 13 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 5 feet straight towards the snareling.


Implosion. When the snareling drops to 0 hit points, it dies as its body implodes.


Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the snareling has disadvantage on attack rolls.


Water Susceptibility. The snareling takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Web Sense. While in contact with a web, the snareling knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.


Web Walker. The snareling ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.


Actions

Multiattack. The snareling makes two Slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage plus 10 (4d4) acid damage.


Teleport. The snareling magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.


Acid Web (Recharge 4-6). Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, range 30/60 ft., one creature. Hit: The target is restrained by webbing. As an action, the restrained target can make a DC 12 Strength check, bursting the webbing on a success. The webbing can also be attacked and destroyed (AC 10; hp 5; vulnerability to cold damage; immunity to acid, bludgeoning, poison, and psychic damage). The target restrained by this web takes 7 (2d6) Acid damage at the start of each of their turns until they are free from the webbing.
