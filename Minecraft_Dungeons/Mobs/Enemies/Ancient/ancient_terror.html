Chilling Aura. Any creature that starts its turn within 30 feet of the wraith must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or have its speed halved until the start of the creature's next turn.


Electrifying Aura. So long as the wraith has at least 1 hit point, each creature within 10 feet of it takes 5 lightning damage at the start of each of the wraith's turns.


Fire Trail. When the wraith moves, it leaves behind a 5-foot-wide line of fire in its path that disappears at the start of its next turn. Each creature that moves through the line must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Heat Aura. Any creature that starts its turn within 5 feet of the wraith takes 3 (1d6) fire damage.


Actions

Life Drain. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d8 + 2) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Consitution saving throw or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0.


Teleport. The wraith magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.


Ice Bouquet (Recharge 5-6). The wraith creates a bouquet of scorching ice in a 10-foot Cube on a point it can see within 60 feet from itself. Each creature in that cube must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 9 (2d8) cold damage plus 9 (2d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
