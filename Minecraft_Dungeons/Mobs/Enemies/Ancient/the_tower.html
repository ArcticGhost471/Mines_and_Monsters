The Tower

Huge tower of Small undead (ancient), neutral evil

Armor Class 13 (natural armor)

Hit Points 95 (10d12 + 30)

Speed 20 ft., fly 20 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
10 (+0) 6 (-2) 16 (+3) 3 (-4) 9 (-1) 5 (-3)

Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 9

Languages —

Challenge 3 (700 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Committed. The tower deals an extra 3 (1d6) force damage with their attack roll to creatures that don't have all of their hit points.


Limited Flight. The tower descends slowly at the end of a turn if it's airborne and the only thing holding it aloft is its flying speed. Descending in this way causes it to take no falling damage.


Stack. The tower can't regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.


Undead Fortitude. If damage reduces the tower to 0 hit points, it must make a Constitution saving throw with a DC of 5 + the damage taken, unless the damage is radiant or from a critical hit. On a success, the tower drops to 1 hit point instead.


Actions

Talons. Melee Weapon Attack: +2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 14 (4d6) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage, or 7 (2d6) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage if the tower has half of its hit points or fewer. If the tower scores a critical hit, it rolls damage dice three times instead of twice.
