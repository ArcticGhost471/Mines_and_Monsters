Deflect. Any time the watchling is targeted by a ranged attack roll, roll a d6. On a 1-5, the attack hits as normal. On a 6, the watchling is unaffected.


Implosion. When the watchling drops to 0 hit points, it dies as its body implodes.


Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the watchling has disadvantage on attack rolls.


Water Susceptibility. The watchling takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Multiattack. The watchling makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.


Teleport. The watchling magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
