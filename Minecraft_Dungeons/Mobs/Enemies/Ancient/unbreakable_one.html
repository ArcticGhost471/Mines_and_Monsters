Committed. The tower deals an extra 3 (1d6) force damage with their attack roll to creatures that don't have all of their hit points.


Electrifying Aura. So long as the guardian has at least 1 hit point, each creature within 10 feet of it takes 15 lightning damage at the start of each of the guardian's turns.


Immutable Form. The golem is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Magic Resistance. The golem has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The golem's weapon attacks are magical.


Actions

Multiattack. The golem makes two slam attacks, or three slam attacks if the golem has half of its hit points or fewer, and uses Thunderous Whirlwind.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +12 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d8 + 6) bludgeoning damage.


Thunderous Whirlwind. The golem magically summons a whirlwind in a 10-foot-high, 10-foot-wide cylinder centered on itself. Each creature in that cylinder must make a DC 20 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (5d8) thunder damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Overload (Recharge 5-6). The golem overloads the redstone in its body, causing a discharge in a 30-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. Each creature in that sphere must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 33 (6d10) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
