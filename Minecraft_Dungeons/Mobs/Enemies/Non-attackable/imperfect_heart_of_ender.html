Cosmic Restoration. When the Heart of Ender submerges itself in a pool of void, the void engulfs its body and reconstructs it in its perfect form, reappearing in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of where it submerged itself. It's initiative count doesn't change.


Divine Being. The Heart of Ender can't be surprised and can't be changed into another form against its will.


Legendary Resistance (1/day in this Form). If the Heart of Ender fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Heart of Ender has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

Blip. Ranged Spell Attack: +13 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d4 + 7) force damage.


Legendary Actions

The Heart of Ender can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Heart of Ender regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Compel Ender. An allied ender creature within 30 feet of the Heart of Ender uses its reaction to make one attack against a target of the Heart of Ender's choice that it can see.


Move. The Heart of Ender moves up to its Speed.


The Heart of Ender's Lair

The Heart of Ender's principal lair is its Broken Citadel, a labyrinthine and layered structure found in the Astral Plane. The city-like architecture is composed by levitating towers and rooms interconnected by bridges and lairs designed to disorient and prevent unwanted visitors from reaching the center of the grand fortress. A large pool of dark void rests at the center of the citadel, contrasted by the pale yellows and purples of the structure, ready to rejuvinate the Heart of Ender's brittle form. Many enderborn that roam the planes of existance are said to originate from this place, and some seek to return to it someday. These creatures, including the Heart of Ender itself, consider the fortress their safe place where no other creature other than them would dare enter.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the Heart of Ender takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the Heart of Ender can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Gravitational Pull. Gravity distorts at a point the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. Any creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that position must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 20 feet into the center and knocked prone.
  • Roots of Oblivion. Grasping tendrils erupt in a 20-foot radius centered on a point on the ground the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. That area becomes difficult terrain, and each creature there must succeed on a DC 21 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the tendrils. A creature can be freed if it or another creature takes an action to make a DC 21 Strength check and succeeds. The tendrils disintegrate when the Heart of Ender uses this lair action again or when the Heart of Ender dies.
  • Spatial Chaos. The Heart of Ender warps space at a point it can see within 120 feet of it. Any creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on that point must succeed on a DC 21 Charisma saving throw or take 21 (6d6) Force damage and be teleported to unnocupied spaces somewhere in the Heart of Ender's lair.

Regional Effect

The region containing the Heart of Ender's lair is warped by its presence, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Dark Chorus. Haunting whispers can be heard throughout the lair. Creatures that can hear the melody have disadvantage on saving throws against being charmed.
  • End Growths. Lavender flowers, yellow trees, and other strange plants grow in abundance within 6 miles of the lair, resembling a forest warped by the a force from far beyond.
  • Floating Islands. Landmassess resembling the ground surrounding them float in the skies within 6 miles of the lair.
  • Void Lakes. Lakes and rivers of liquid void can be encountered sparcely within 6 miles of the lair. Entering the void causes creatures to take 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

If the Heart of Ender dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.
