The Heart of Ender's Lair

The Heart of Ender's principal lair is its Broken Citadel, a labyrinthine and layered structure found in the Astral Plane. The city-like architecture is composed by levitating towers and rooms interconnected by bridges and lairs designed to disorient and prevent unwanted visitors from reaching the center of the grand fortress. A large pool of dark void rests at the center of the citadel, contrasted by the pale yellows and purples of the structure, ready to rejuvinate the Heart of Ender's brittle form. Many ender creatures that roam the planes of existance are said to originate from this place, and some seek to return to it someday. These creatures, including the Heart of Ender itself, consider the fortress their safe place where no other creature other than them would dare enter.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the Heart of Ender takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the Heart of Ender can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Gravitational Pull. Gravity distorts at a point the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. Any creature in a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on that position must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 20 feet into the center and knocked prone.
  • Roots of Oblivion. Grasping tendrils erupt in a 20-foot radius centered on a point on the ground the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. That area becomes difficult terrain, and each creature there must succeed on a DC 15 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the tendrils. A creature can be freed if it or another creature takes an action to make a DC 15 Strength check and succeeds. The tendrils disintegrate when the Heart of Ender uses this lair action again or when the Heart of Ender dies.
  • Shulker Pellet. Shulker pellets are conjured and shot by shulkers hidden in the walls of the lair, targetting up to three creatures that the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet. The Heart of Ender makes one ranged attack roll (+7 to hit) against each target. On a hit, the target takes 10 (3d6) force damage and rises vertically up to 20 feet until initiative count 20 on the next round. The target takes fall damage when the effects ends, otherwise the effect is identical to the levitate spell.

Regional Effect

The region containing the Heart of Ender's lair is warped by his magic, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • End Growths. Lavender flowers, yellow trees, and other strange plants grow in abundance within 6 miles of the lair, resembling a forest warped by the a force from far beyond.
  • Floating Islands. Landmassess resembling the ground surrounding them float in the skies within 6 miles of the lair.
  • Void Lakes. Lakes and rivers of liquid void can be encountered sparcely within 6 miles of the lair. Entering the void causes creatures to take 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

If the Heart of Ender dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.
