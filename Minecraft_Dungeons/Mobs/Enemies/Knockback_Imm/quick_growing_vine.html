Variant: Quick Growing Kelp

In underwater enviroments, plant-life similar to the land dwelling vines grow. Though physically weaker, quick growing kelps strike at intruders to their habitat in the same vein as the quick growing vines.

 A quick growing kelp has the same statistics of a quick growing vine in addition to the following additional trait.

 Water Breathing. The vine can breathe only underwater.
