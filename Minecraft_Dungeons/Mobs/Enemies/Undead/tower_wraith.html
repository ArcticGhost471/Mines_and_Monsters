Tower Wraith

Small undead (tower keeper), neutral evil

Armor Class 13

Hit Points 44 (8d6 + 16)

Speed 0 ft., fly 60 ft. (hover)

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
6 (-2) 16 (+3) 14 (+2) 12 (+1) 16 (+3) 15 (+2)

Damage Resistances acid, fire, lightning, thunder; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical weapons that aren't silvered

Damage Immunities cold, necrotic, poison, radiant

Condition Immunities charmed, exhaustion, grappled, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, prone, restrained

Senses darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13

Languages Understands all languages it knew in life but can't speak

Challenge 3 (700 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Actions

Life Drain. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (3d8 + 3) necrotic damage. The target must succeed on a DC 13 Consitution saving throw or its hit point maximum is reduced by an amount equal to the damage taken. This reduction lasts until the target finishes a long rest. The target dies if this effect reduces its hit point maximum to 0.


Teleport. The wraith magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.


Ice Bouquet (Black Robes Only; Recharge 5-6). The wraith creates a bouquet of scorching ice in a 15-foot Cube on a point it can see within 60 feet from itself. Each creature in that cube must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 13 (3d8) cold damage plus 13 (3d8) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.


Poison Cloud (Pink Robes Only; Recharge 5-6). The wraith creates a cloud of noxious gas in a 20-foot Sphere centered on a point it can see within 60 feet from itself. Each creature in that sphere must make a DC 13 Constitution saving throw, taking 24 (7d6) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.


Bonus Actions

Robe Switch. The wraith magically switches the color of their robe from black to pink, and viceversa, along its magical effects. Action recharges are shared between robes.
