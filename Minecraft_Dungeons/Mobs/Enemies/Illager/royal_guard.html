Variant: Tower Guard

Within the tower keeper's Challenge Spire, the tower keeper has recruited a set of royal guards to work for them. These Tower Guards protect the Challenge Spire and participate in the tower keeper's games whenever someone wishes to take on the spire.

 A tower guard has a challenge rating of 4 (1,100 XP). It has the same statistics of an illager royal guard except that it replaces the royal guard's action options with the following action options.
