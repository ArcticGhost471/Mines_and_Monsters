Spellcasting. The illager is a 18th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit with spell attacks). The illager has the following spells prepared:


Cantrips (at will): light, mending, spare the dying, thaumaturgy, toll the dead

1st level (4 slots): cure wounds, detect evil and good, guiding bolt, sanctuary

2nd level (3 slots): aid, lesser restoration, spiritual weapon

3rd level (3 slots): dispel magic, protection from energy, revivify

4th level (3 slots): freedom of movement, guardian of fate, locate creature

5th level (2 slots): greater restoration, mass cure wounds, raise dead

6th level (1 slots): create undead

7th level (1 slots): regenerate

8th level (1 slots): holy aura

9th level (1 slots): gate


Actions

Dagger. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.


Fang Stream (Recharge 5-6). The illager magically summons a wave of ethereal fangs that erupt from the ground in a 5-foot-wide, 30-foot-long line. Each creature in the line must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 40 (9d8) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Summon Vexes (1/day). The illager magically summons three vexes. The vexes appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the illager, whom they obey. They take their turn immediately after the illager. They last for 1 hour, until they or the illager dies, or until the illager dismisses them as a bonus action.


Reactions

Fang Retaliation (Recharge 5-6). If a creature ends their turn within 10 feet of the illager, the illager magically summons a wave of ethereal fangs that erupt from the ground within a 10-foot-radius area centered on itself. Each creature in the area must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (5d8) piercing damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
