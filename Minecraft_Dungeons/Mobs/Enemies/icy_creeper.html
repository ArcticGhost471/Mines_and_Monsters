Bizzare Physiology. The creeper can't take actions except for Dash, Disengage, Hide, Search. It can't take bonus actions or reactions.


Fear of Felines. The creeper has the frightened condition whenever it starts its turn within 60 feet of a feline creature it can see.


Ignition. When it ends its turn within 10 feet of a Humanoid that it can see, the creeper stops moving and emits a hiss loud enough to be heard by creatures within 30 feet of itself.

If the creeper drops to 0 hit points before the start of its next turn, the hissing stops, and the creeper dies. Otherwise, at the start of the creeper's next turn, one of the following things happens:

  • If there are no Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, it stops hissing. It then uses any available movement to approach the nearest Humanoid it can see.
  • If there are one or more Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, the creeper explodes in a bubble of supercooled water that fills a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. The creeper is destroyed, and every other creature in the explosion's area must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. On a failed save, the target's movement speed is halved until the end of its next turn. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take 7 (2d6) cold damage.
