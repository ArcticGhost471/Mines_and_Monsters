Divine Being. The Heart of Ender can't be surprised and can't be changed into another form against its will.


Legendary Resistance (3/day in this Form). If the Heart of Ender fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The Heart of Ender has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Actions

Multiattack. The Heart of Ender makes two slam attacks and one cosmic flare attack.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +11 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 15 (2d10 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage.


Cosmic Flare. Ranged Spell Attack: +14 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 26 (4d12) fire damage.


Beguile (3/day). The Heart of Ender telepathically whispers to one creature within range of its telepathy, preying on their fears and desires. The creature must succeed on a DC 22 Wisdom saving throw or be magically charmed by the Heart of Ender until the Heart of Ender dies or until it is on a different plane of existence from the target. The charmed target is under the Heart of Ender's control and the Heart of Ender and the target can communicate telepathically with each other over any distance. The charmed target obeys to the best of its ability any command the Heart of Ender issues that isn't directly harmful to the target.

 Whenever the charmed target takes damage, the target can repeat the saving throw. On a success, the effect ends. No more than once every 24 hours, the target can also repeat the saving throw when it is at least 6 miles away from the Heart of Ender.


Void Breath (Recharge 5-6). The Heart of Ender exhales astral nothing in a 90-foot cone. Each creature in the cone must make a DC 22 Constitution saving throw, taking 67 (15d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Legendary Actions

The Heart of Ender can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The Heart of Ender regains spent legendary actions at the start of their turn.


Cosmic Flare. The Heart of Ender makes one cosmic flare attack.

Move. The Heart of Ender moves up to its speed.

Astral Beam (Costs 2 Actions). The Heart of Ender fires a beam of decaying arcana in a 90-foot Line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must succeed on a DC 22 Dexterity saving throw or take 14 (4d6) necrotic damage.


The Heart of Ender's Lair

The Heart of Ender's principal lair is its Broken Citadel, a labyrinthine and layered structure found in the Astral Plane. The city-like architecture is composed by levitating towers and rooms interconnected by bridges and layers designed to disorient unwanted visitors to prevent them from reaching the center of the grand fortress. A large pool of dark void rests at the center of the citadel, contrasted by the pale yellows and purples of the structure, ready to rejuvinate the Heart of Ender's brittle form. Many enderborn that roam the planes of existance are said to originate from this place, and some seek to return to it someday. These creatures, including the Heart of Ender itself, consider the fortress their safe place where no other creature other than them would dare enter.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the Heart of Ender takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the Heart of Ender can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Gravitational Pull. Gravity distorts within 60-foot radius sphere centered on the Heart of Ender. Any creature within that sphere must succeed on a DC 22 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 30 feet into the center and knocked prone.
  • Roots of Oblivion. Grasping tendrils erupt in a 20-foot radius centered on a point on the ground the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. That area becomes difficult terrain, and each creature there must succeed on a DC 22 Strength saving throw or be restrained by the tendrils. A creature can be freed if it or another creature takes an action to make a DC 22 Strength check and succeeds. The tendrils disintegrate when the Heart of Ender uses this lair action again or when the Heart of Ender dies.
  • Spatial Chaos. The Heart of Ender warps space at a point it can see within 120 feet of it. Any creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on that point must succeed on a DC 22 Charisma saving throw or take 21 (6d6) Force damage and be teleported to unnocupied spaces somewhere in the Heart of Ender's lair.

Regional Effect

The region containing the Heart of Ender's lair is warped by its presence, creating one or more of the following effects:

  • Dark Chorus. Haunting whispers can be heard throughout the lair. Creatures that can hear the melody have disadvantage on saving throws against being charmed.
  • End Wilds. Lavender flowers, yellow trees, and other strange plants grow in abundance within 6 miles of the lair, resembling a forest warped by the a force from far beyond.
  • Floating Islands. Portions of the land tear free and drift upward, forming floating islands within 6 miles of the lair.
  • Void Lakes. Lakes and rivers of liquid void can be encountered sparcely within 6 miles of the lair. Entering the void causes creatures to take 7 (2d6) necrotic damage.

If the Heart of Ender dies, these effects fade over the course of 1d10 days.

Legendary Actions

Legendary Action Uses: 3 (4 in Lair). Immediately after another creature's turn, the Heart of Ender can expend a use to take one of the following actions. the Heart of Ender regains all expended uses at the start of each of their turns.


Astral Beam. Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 22, each creature in a 90-foot-long, 5-foot-wide Line. Failure: 22 (4d10) Necrotic damage, and the target's Hit Point maximum decreases by an amount equal to the damage taken. Failure or Success: The Heart of Ender can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Spatial Chaos. Charisma Saving Throw: DC 22, each creature in a 20-foot radius sphere centered on a point the Heart of Ender can see within 120 feet of it. Failure: The creature takes 21 (6d6) Force damage and is teleported to an unoccupied space somewhere in the Heart of Ender's lair. Failure or Success: The Heart of Ender can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.


Wrathful Slam. The Heart of Ender moves up to half its Speed and makes one Slam attack.


The Heart of Ender's Lair

The Heart of Ender's lair is its Broken Citadel, a city-like fortress found in the Astral Plane that it considers its safe place.


Regional Effects

The region containing the Heart of Ender's lair is warped by its presence, creating the following effects:


Aberrant Thicket. Strange, lavender and yellow plants grow within 1 mile of the lair, emitting ominous choruses and whispers. Any creature (excluding the Heart of Ender and its allies) that finishes a Short Rest in that area must succeed on a DC 22 Wisdom saving throw or gain no benefit from that rest and have Disadvantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Charmed condition.

Void Lakes. Lakes and rivers of liquid void can be encountered sparcely within 6 miles of the lair. Creatures entering the liquid void must succeed on a DC 22 Constitution saving throw or take 7 (2d6) necrotic damage and regurgitate the liquid.


If the Heart of Ender dies or moves its lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
