Incorporeal Movement. The wraith can move through other creatures and objects as if they were Difficult Terrain. It takes 5 (1d10) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.


Actions

Multiattack. The wraith makes three ghostfire burst attacks


Ghostfire Burst. Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +8 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (4d8 + 4) cold damage.


Teleport. The wraith magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.


Shadow Barrage (Recharge 5-6). The wraith unleashes a barrage of necrotic energy in a 60-foot Cone. Each creature in that cube must make a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw, taking 63 (14d8) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.


Wretched Wraith's Lair

Wretched Wraith dominates Frosted Fjord, a frozen island where the Lone Fortress resides. With otherwordly power, the Wretched Wraith engulfs the island in an eternal winter and causes hordes of undead to rise from their graves.


Regional Effects

The region containing Wretched Wraith's lair is warped by its presence, creating the following effects


Frigid Cold. The area within 6 mile of the lair is an area of extreme cold. See the Dungeon Master's Guide for rules on extreme cold.

Frozen Rivers. Water within 6 miles of the lair is frigid water. See the Dungeon Master's Guide for rules on frigid water.

Land of Undeath. The area within 1 mile of the lair is desecrated ground. See the Dungeon Master's Guide for rules on desecrated ground.


If Wretched Wraith dies or moves its lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
