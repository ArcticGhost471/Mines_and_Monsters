Proficiency Bonus +5


Immutable Form. The monstrosity is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Magic Resistance. The monstrosity has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Magic Weapons. The monstrosity's weapon attacks are magical.


Siege Monster. The monstrosity deals double damage to objects and structures.


Actions

Multiattack. The monstrosity makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +14 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target. Hit: 21 (3d8 + 8) bludgeoning damage.


Burning Mushrooms (Recharge 5-6). The monstrosity expels burning mushrooms from its hands in a 90-foot Cone. Each creature in that cone must make a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw, taking 42 (12d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Mycelium Herd (1/day). The monstrosity magically summons five mooshrooms. The summoned mooshrooms appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the monstrosity and take their turns immediately after it in the initiative order. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or the monstrosity dies, or until the monstrosity dismisses it as a bonus action.
