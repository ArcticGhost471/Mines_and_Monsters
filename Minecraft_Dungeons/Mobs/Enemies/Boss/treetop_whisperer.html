Treetop Whisperer

Large plant, unaligned

Armor Class 15 (natural armor)

Hit Points 85 (10d10 + 30)

Speed 20 ft.

STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
15 (+2) 8 (-1) 16 (+3) 3 (-4) 10 (+0) 6 (-2)

Condition Immunities blinded, deafened

Senses blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius), passive Perception 10

Languages —

Challenge 3 (700 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Innate Spellcasting (1/day). The whisperer can innately cast wall of thorns (spell save DC 13), requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Constitution.


Regeneration. The whisperer regains 10 hit points at the start of its turn. If the whisperer takes acid or fire damage, this trait doesn't function at the start of the whisperer's next turn.


Actions

Constrict. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage, and a Large or smaller target is Grappled (escape DC 13). Until the grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the whisperer can't constrict another target.


Entangling Plants (Recharge 5-6). Grasping roots and vines sprout in a 15-foot radius centered on the whisperer, withering away after one minute. For the duration, the area is difficult terrain for nonplant creatures. In addition, each creature of the whisperer's choice in that area must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or become restrained. A creature can use its action to make a DC 13 Strength check, freeing itself or another entangled creature within reach on a success.


Bonus Actions

Summon Poison Quill (1/day). The whisperer magically summons five poison-quill vine. The summoned vines appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the whisperer and take their turns immediately after it in the initiative order. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or the whisperer dies, or until the whisperer dismisses it as a bonus action.
