Cosmic Ressurection. When Archie drops to 0 hit points, his body is liberated from the Heart of Ender's influence. The Orb of Dominance then shatters and the Heart's divine spark vanishes. It is dead until one of the Orb's shards is recovered and returned to the Astral Plane, where the Heart of Ender reappears at full health in its imperfect form.


Magic Resistance. Archie has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Vessel Trail. When Archie moves, he leaves behind a 5-foot-wide line of astral flame in his path that disappears at the start of his next turn. Each creature that moves through the line must make a DC 17 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (3d6) Necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.


Actions

Multiattack. Archie makes two slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d10 + 2) bludgeoning damage plus 11 (2d10) necrotic damage.


Voidfire Flame. Ranged Spell Attack: +11 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d8) fire damage.


Entropic Breath (Recharge 5-6). Archie exhales astral flame in a 60-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in the line must make a DC 16 Constitution saving throw, taking 45 (13d6) necrotic damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
