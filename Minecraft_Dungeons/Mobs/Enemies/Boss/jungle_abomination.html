Actions

Multiattack. The abomination makes two slam attacks.

Grasping Vine. Melee Weapon Attack: +9 to hit, reach 30 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (2d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage, and the target is Grappled (escape DC 17). Until the grapple ends, the target is restrained, and it takes 13 (2d6 + 6) bludgeoning damage at the start of each of its turns.


Hunters of the Jungle (1/day). The abomination magically calls 1d4 whisperers or 1d4 - 2 (minimum one). The called creatures arrive in 1d4 rounds, acting as allies of the abomination. The plants remain for 1 hour, until the abomination dies, or until the abomination dismisses them as a bonus action.


Dart Burst (Recharge 5-6). The abomination discharges a series of poison darts in a 90-foot Cone. Each creature in that cone must make a DC 17 Dexterity saving throw, taking 30 (12d4) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.
