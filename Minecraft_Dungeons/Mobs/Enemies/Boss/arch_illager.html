Innate Spellcasting. Archie's spellcasting ability is Charisma. He can innately cast the following spells (spell save DC 14), requiring no material components:


At will: detect magic, levitate, mage armor (self only)

1/day: bestow curse


Spellcasting. Archie is a 10th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 14, +6 to hit with spell attacks). He regains his expended spell slots when he finishes a short or long rest. He knows the following warlock spells:


Cantrips (at will): chill touch, eldritch blast, minor illusion, prestidigitation

1st-5th level (3 5th-level slots): armor of Agathys, crown of madness, clairvoyance, contact other plane, dimension door, dominate beast, fly, hold monster, ray of enfeeblement, Tasha's hideous laughter, unseen servant, vampiric touch


Orb of Dominance. The orb has 7 charges, and any of its properties that require a saving throw have a save DC of 18. While holding it, Archie can use an action to cast detect thoughts, dissonant whispers, or speak with dead. Alternatively, he can expend 1 or more of the orb's charges to cast one of the following spells from it: disintegrate (1 charge), telekinesis (1 charge), or dominate monster (2 charges). The orb regains 1d4 + 3 charges daily at dawn.

 While holding the Orb, Archie can use an action to conjure humanoid (testificate) creatures whose combined average hit points don't exceed 200. These humanoids magically materialize in unoccupied spaces within 120 feet of Archie and obey his commands until they die or until he dismisses them as an action. Once this property of the Orb is used, the property can't be used again until the next dawn.

 The Orb of Dominance has AC 15, HP 20, and Resistance to all damage. The orb shatters if reduced to 0 Hit Points.


Vessel Possession. When Archie drops to 0 hit points, his body is enveloped by a whirlwind and absorbed into the Orb of Dominance. The Orb of Dominance vanishes alongside Archie. Archie instantly reappears as a vessel for the Heart of Ender, in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of where he vanished. His initiative count doesn't change.


Actions

Multiattack. Archie makes three end bolt attacks.


End Bolt. Ranged Spell Attack: +6 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (4d6 + 3) necrotic damage.


Staff of Dominance. Melee Weapon Attack: +5 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage, or 11 (2d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage when used with two hands.
