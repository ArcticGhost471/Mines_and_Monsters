Challenge 1/2 (100 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


False Appearance. While the whisperer remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal plant.


Innate Spellcasting (1/day). The whisperer can innately cast wall of thorns (spell save DC 12), requiring no material components. Its innate spellcasting ability is Constitution.


Actions

Constrict. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (2d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage, and a Large or smaller target is Grappled (escape DC 12). Until the grapple ends, the target is restrained, and the whisperer can't constrict another target.


Entangling Plants (Recharge 5-6). Grasping roots and vines sprout in a 15-foot radius centered on the whisperer, withering away after one minute. For the duration, the area is difficult terrain for nonplant creatures. In addition, each creature of the whisperer's choice in that area must succeed on a DC 12 Strength saving throw or become restrained. A creature can use its action to make a DC 12 Strength check, freeing itself or another entangled creature within reach on a success.


Bonus Actions

Summon Poison Quill (1/day). The whisperer magically summons five poison-quill vine. The summoned vines appear in unoccupied spaces within 60 feet of the whisperer and take their turns immediately after it in the initiative order. Each lasts for 1 hour, until it or the whisperer dies, or until the whisperer dismisses it as a bonus action.


Variant: Wave Whisperer

The magic wielding whisperer have been seen roaming the depths of the ocean. Known as the wave whisperers, this underwater variant reside within the ocean depths and ambush unsuspecting victims, dragging them down alongside it at the bottom.

 A Wave Whisperer has the same statistics of a whisperer in addition to the following additional trait.

 Water Breathing. The whisperer can breathe only underwater.
