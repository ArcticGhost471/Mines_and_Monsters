Poisonous Spines. While puffed, the pufferfish deals 5 (1d10) plus 5 (2d4) poison damage to any creature grappling it or that hits it with a melee attack.


Water Breathing. The pufferfish can breathe only underwater.


Actions

Bite. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage or 9 (2d6 + 2) piercing damage while puffed.


Bonus Action

Puff Up (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest). For 1 minute, the pufferfish puffs up, increasing its size by filling its stomach with air or water. While puffed up, the pufferfish is Small, doubles its damage dice on attacks, and it extends its spines. If the pufferfish lacks the room to become Small, it attains the maximum size possible in the space available.
