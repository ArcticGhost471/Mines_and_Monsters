Variants: Stone Ministrosity and Glowing Ministrosity

Ministrosities can be constructed without redstone being involved in the process of its creation. These ministrosities made of pure stone are known as Stone Ministrosities, they lack the iconic redstone touch of their bretheren yet they are as friendly as them. Some of these ministrosities are able to delevop fluorescence, allowing them to absorb ultra-violet light and gain the ability to glow and illuminate the world around them.

 A stone ministrosity loses its resistance to lightning damage. Otherwise it has the same statistics as a redstone ministrosity.

 A glowing ministrosity loses its resistance to lightning damage, and gains the following additional trait.


 Illumination. The ministrosity sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.
