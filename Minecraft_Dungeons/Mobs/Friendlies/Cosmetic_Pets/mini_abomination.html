Variants: Glowing Abomination

Sometimes the materials used in the creation of a mini abomination can give them unique properties. The use of fluorescent or glowing flora like glow berries can cause the mini abominations to adopt their properties. Glowing mini abominations are more attuned to nature than regular mini abominations, dedicating their existance to learning and caring for the forests they are made of.

 A glowing mini abomination has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet, as well as the following additional trait.


 Illumination. The mini abomination sheds bright light in a 5-foot radius and dim light for an additional 5 feet.
