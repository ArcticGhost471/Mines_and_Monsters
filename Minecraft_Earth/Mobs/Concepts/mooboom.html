Forced Ignition. When the mooboom is subjected to fire damage, the mooboom ignites and emits a hiss loud enough to be heard by creatures within 30 feet of itself. At the start of its next turn, the mooboom explodes in a ball of fire that fills a 30-foot-radius sphere cenetered on itself. The mooboom is destroyed, and every other creature in the explosion's area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take 17 (5d6) fire damage.
