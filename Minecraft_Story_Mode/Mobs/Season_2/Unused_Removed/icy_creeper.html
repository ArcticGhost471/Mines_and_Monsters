Cold Absorption. Whenever the creeper is subjected to cold damage, it takes no damage and instead regains a number of hit points equal to the cold damage dealt.


Ignition. When it ends its turn within 10 feet of a Humanoid that it can see, the creeper stops moving and emits a hiss loud enough to be heard by creatures within 30 feet of itself.

If the creeper drops to 0 hit points before the start of its next turn, the hissing stops, and the creeper dies. Otherwise, at the start of the creeper's next turn, one of the following things happens:

  • If there are no Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, it stops hissing. It then uses any available movement to approach the nearest Humanoid it can see.
  • If there are one or more Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, the creeper explodes in a ball of energy that fills a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. The creeper is destroyed, and every other creature in the explosion's area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 7 (2d6) Cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take 7 (2d6) Cold damage.

Melt. While in an area of extreme heat, the creeper loses 1d6 hit points at the start of each of its turns.


Water Susceptibility. The creeper takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Snowball. Ranged Weapon Attack: +0 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 3 (1d6) cold damage.
