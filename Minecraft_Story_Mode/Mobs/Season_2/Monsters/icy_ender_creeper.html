Ignition. When it ends its turn within 10 feet of a Humanoid that it can see, the creeper stops moving and emits a hiss loud enough to be heard by creatures within 30 feet of itself.

If the creeper drops to 0 hit points before the start of its next turn, the hissing stops, and the creeper dies. Otherwise, at the start of the creeper's next turn, one of the following things happens:

  • If there are no Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, it stops hissing. It then uses any available movement to approach the nearest Humanoid it can see.
  • If there are one or more Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeper, the creeper explodes in a ball of energy that fills a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. The creeper is destroyed, and every other creature in the explosion's area must make a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw, taking 17 (5d6) cold damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take 17 (5d6) cold damage.

Sunlight Sensitivity. While in sunlight, the creeper has disadvantage on attack rolls.


Water Susceptibility. The creeper takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Multiattack. The creeper makes two Slam attacks.


Slam. Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (1d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) cold damage.


Teleport. The creeper magically teleports, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
