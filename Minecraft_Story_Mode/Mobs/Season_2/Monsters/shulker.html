Condition Immunities charmed, exhaustion, frightened, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned

Senses darkvision 120 ft., passive Perception 10

Languages —

Challenge 2 (450 XP)

Proficiency Bonus +2


Chameleon Skin. The creature has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide.


Immutable Form. The shulker is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Magic Resistance. The shulker has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Water Susceptibility. The shulker takes 1 acid damage for every 5 feet it moves in water, for every gallon of water splashed on it, or whenever it starts its turn in the rain.


Actions

Shulker Bullet. The shulker conjures a magical spark and sends it after a creature or object it can see. The spark moves 20 feet towards the target by the most direct route at the end of the shulker's turn. A spark is a Tiny object that has AC 10; 1 hit point; and immunity to poison and psychic damage. When a spark reaches the target, it bursts dealing 5 (2d4) force damage, the target is raised vertically up to 10 feet, and remains suspended there until the end of the shulker's next turn. When the effect ends, the target falls, unless it can hover or is supported. The spark remains for 1 minute, until it hits a solid surface, such as a wall or ceiling, or is reduced to 0 HP. The shulker can have up to 3 active sparks at once.


Reactions

Reactive Teleportation. Whenever it takes damage from a water source or is physically prevented from opening its shell (such as being Restrained), the shulker can teleport, along with any equipment it is wearing or carrying, to any unoccupied space within 20 feet of itself.


Shulker Shell. If the shulker takes an action this turn, it hides within its shell and adds 10 to its AC until the end of its next turn. While it remains hidden, it cannot take actions or reactions, except for Reactive Teleportation.
