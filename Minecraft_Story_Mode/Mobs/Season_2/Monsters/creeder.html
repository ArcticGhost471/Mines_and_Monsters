Ignition. When it ends its turn within 10 feet of a Humanoid that it can see, the creeder stops moving and emits a hiss loud enough to be heard by creatures within 30 feet of itself.

If the creeder drops to 0 hit points before the start of its next turn, the hissing stops, and the creeder dies. Otherwise, at the start of the creeder's next turn, one of the following things happens:

  • If there are no Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeder, it stops hissing. It then uses any available movement to approach the nearest Humanoid it can see.
  • If there are one or more Humanoids within 10 feet of the hissing creeder, the creeder explodes in a ball of energy that fills a 20-foot-radius sphere centered on itself. The creeder is destroyed, and every other creature in the explosion's area must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 21 (6d6) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one. Objects in the area that aren't being worn or carried take 21 (6d6) lightning damage.

Spider Climb. The creeder can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceiling, without needing to make an ability check.


Web Sense. While in contact with a web, the creeder knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.


Web Walker. The creeder ignores movement restrictions caused by webbing.


Actions

Multiattack. White eyes makes two foreleg attacks.


Foreleg. Melee Weapon Attack: +3 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 8 (2d6 + 1) piercing damage.
