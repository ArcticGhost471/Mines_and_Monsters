Actions

Multiattack. The admin makes three shortsword attacks.


Shortsword. Melee Weapon Attack: +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 16 (4d6 + 2) piercing damage.


Bonus Actions

Summon Golem (1/day). The admin magically summons a prison golem. The summoned golem appears in an unoccupied space within 60 feet of the admin, whom they obey. It takes its turn immediately after the admin. They remain for 1 hour, until it or the admin dies, or until the admin dismisses it as a bonus action.


Legendary Actions

The admin can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The admin regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Command Golem. An allied construct within 30 feet of the admin uses its reaction to make one attack against a target of the admin's choice that it can see.

Shortsword. The admin makes one shortsword attack.

Cast a spell (Costs 2 Actions). The admin uses spellcasting.


An Admin's Lair

An admin's makes their lairs in any man-made location, such as a mightly fortress, an isolated tower, or a village farmhouse. Some attempt to blend in within populated settlements, not bringing much attention to themselves and focusing on achieving their personal goals; others choose to stand out, showcasing their might with spectacle or chaos.


Lair Actions

On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), the admin takes a lair action to cause one of the following effects; the admin can't use the same effect two rounds in a row:

  • Administrative Clap. The admin claps, releasing a wave of arcane energy in a 20-foot radius sphere at a point it can see within 120 feet of it. Each creature in that sphere mush make a successful DC 15 Constitution saving throw or take 10 (3d6) force damage.
  • Burying Rubble. The admin forms a 10-foot cube of rubble and telekinetically hurls it at a point it can see within 120 feet of it. Each creature in that area must make a successful DC 15 Dexterity saving throw or take 10 (3d6) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone and buried. The buried target is restrained and unable to breathe or stand up. A creature can take an action to make a DC 10 Strength check, ending the buried state on a success.
  • Crumbling Floor. Part of the floor collapses in a 20-foot radius centered on a point on the ground the admin can see within 120 feet of it. Each creature in that area is magically held aloft by the admin and is moved to the nearest unoccupied space from their current position. The floor is repaired when the admin uses this lair action again or when the admin dies.

Regional Effect

The region containing an admin's lair is warped by the admin's magic, which creates one or more of the following effects:

  • Construct Followers. The admin excerts influence over constructs in its lair. After each short or long rest, Large or smaller constructs must make a successful DC 15 Charisma saving throw or become charmed while within 1 mile of the lair.
  • Tinted Skies. The skies within 1 mile of the lair is tinted in a color of the admin's choice.
  • Unnatural Weather. The weather within 1 mile of the lair is unnatural within its environment. Constant thunderstorms, snow, fog, or clear skies surround the lair, even in contrast to the weather beyond the lair. The admin may choose to change its lair's weather after a long rest.

If the admin dies, these effects end after 2d6 days.

Admin Lairs

Admins make their bastions within cities or in isolation. Some don't hide their lairs, and often become the center of attention within their chosen settlement; others prefer solitary lifestyles, hiding their lairs in places where one wouldn't check at first glance.
