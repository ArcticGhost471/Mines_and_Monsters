Controlled Chaos. The wither is under the effects of a powerful charm from the moment it is created. While charmed in this way, the wither is forced to follow any command its creator gives it. In addition, the wither's Rapid Growth trait is suppressed as long as it remains charmed. The effect lasts for 24 hours or until its creator dies, is on a different plane of existance from its creator, or its creator ends the effect as a bonus action.


Immutable Form. The wither is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (1/day in This Stage). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Rapid Growth. The wither begins absorbing anything in its vicinity. If the wither has at least 1 hit point after 1 minute, the wither grows into its second stage, transferring any damage taken into this stage. It's initiative count doesn't change.


Spawn Wave. When the wither is created, it unleashes a shockwave around itself. Each creature within 15 feet of it must make a successful DC 16 Strength saving throw or be pushed up to 15 feet away from the wither if it is Huge or smaller.


Three Heads. The wither has advantage on Wisdom (perception) checks and on saving throws against being blinded, charmed, deafened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.


Actions

Burning Skull. Ranged Spell Attack: +4 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 13 (3d8) fire damage plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage.


Legendary Actions

The wither can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wither regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Detect. The wither makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

Move. The wither moves up to half its speed.

Burning Skull (Costs 2 Actions). The wither makes one burning skull attack.
