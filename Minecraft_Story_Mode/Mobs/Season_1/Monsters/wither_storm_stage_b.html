Immutable Form. The wither is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.


Legendary Resistance (3/day in This Stage). If the wither fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead.


Magic Resistance. The wither has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.


Siege Monster. The wither deals double damage to objects and structures.


Three Heads. The wither has advantage on Wisdom (perception) checks and on saving throws against being blinded, deafened, stunned, and knocked unconscious.


Uncontrolled Growth. The wither uses its tractor beams to absorb anything it can see. If the wither has at least 1 hit point after 24 hours, the wither grows into its third stage, transferring any damage taken into this stage. It's initiative count doesn't change.


Actions

Multiattack. The wither makes three burning skull attacks.


Burning Skull. Ranged Spell Attack: +9 to hit, range 60 ft., one target. Hit: 22 (5d8) fire damage plus 4 (1d8) necrotic damage.


Tentacle. Melee Weapon Attack: +10 to hit, reach 20 ft., one target. Hit: 17 (3d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 19) if its a Huge or smaller creature. Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained. The wither has five tentacles, each of which can grapple one target. If the wither scores a critical hit against a creature, the target must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw or become diseased. The disease has no effect for 1 minute and can be removed by any magic that cures disease. After 1 minute, the diseased creature's skin becomes pale, an area of their skin decays, darkening to a purple hue, the creature can't regain hit points, and the disease can be removed only by wish or the death of the wither that inflicted it. After 10 minutes, the diseased creature becomes poisoned. While the creature is affected by this disease, it takes 5 (1d10) necrotic damage every 10 minutes. If the disease reduces the target's hit points to 0, the target dies, and its body rapidly darkens with decay and their eyes and mouth glow with an unnatural violet hue.


Tractor Gaze. The wither uses its three tractor beams, choosing one to three targets it can see within 90 feet of it. If the target is a creature, it must succeed on a DC 19 Strength saving throw or be pulled up to 15 feet straight towards the wither.


Legendary Actions

The wither can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. The wither regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.


Move. The wither moves up to half its speed.

Tentacle. The wither makes one tentacle attack.

Tractor Gaze (Costs 2 Actions). The wither uses its tractor gaze.

Tentacle. Melee Attack Roll: +10, reach 20 ft. Hit: 17 (3d8 + 4) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Huge or smaller creature, the target has the Grappled condition (escape DC 19) from one of five tentacles, and it has the Restrained condition until the grapple ends. If the wither scores a critical hit against a creature, it is also cursed. While cursed, the target can't regain Hit Points, its skin becomes pale, it has the Poisoned condition, and it takes 10 (3d6) Necrotic damage every 24 hours that elapse. A creature dies and decays if reduced to 0 Hit Points by this attack.


Tractor Gaze. Strength Saving Throw: DC 19, one creature the wither can see within 90 feet (the target succeeds automatically if it is Gargantuan). Failure: The target is pulled up to 30 feet straight towards the wither.


Legendary Actions

Lash. The wither makes one Tentacle attack.


Tractor Gaze. The wither uses its Tractor Gaze. The wither can't take this action again until the start of its next turn.
